Title: Using UT4 XLR and 1/4" output simultaneously.
Id: 1033
Legacy Id: 1064

Question:
I'm thinking of purchasing this unit, but I need to know if the UT4 receiver can send output signals from the XLR and 1/4" jack simultaneously as I need to send one signal to my amp and another to my tuner.


Answer:

	Yes, you may use both the XLR and 1/4jack at the same time.
