Title: M44-7x cartridge and replacement needles
Id: 1039
Legacy Id: 1071

Question:
I've got the old M44-7X cartridges and I've run out of replacement needles for them. Will the new N44-7 and N44GX replacement needles work with them?


Answer:

	Sorry, the newer styli will not fit into this older M44-7X cartridge.
