Title: SCM262 mixer output polarity
Id: 1045
Legacy Id: 1078

Question:
We have a Shure SCM262 mic/line mixer. There seems to be no reference to this in the manual. The TRS main output jacks; what is their polarity? Which contact is HOT or +? The TIP OR RING?


Answer:

	The tip is positive, ring negative, and sleeve ground.
