Title: Replacing T1P Transmitter
Id: 1067
Legacy Id: 1102

Question:
I have a T4 receiver that have a frequency of 177.600 MHz. and a T1P(old) bodypack transmitter that have a frquency CC 177.600 MHz. Can I replace the T1P transmitter with the new T1 transmitter? Does this transmitter have to be in the same frequency with the receiver? Thanks!




Answer:

	Yes and Yes.
	
	The T1 transmitter can be used with the receiver. Yes, it can be purchased on the same frequency.

	

	The T series is now discontinued and transmitters are no longer available.
