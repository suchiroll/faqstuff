Title: Simplex Power on M267 mixer
Id: 1194
Legacy Id: 1238

Question:
Couldn't get a hit on "SIMPLEX" the manual talks about phantom power (switch etc.) My M267 says SIMPLEX on/off. Are they the same?


Answer:

	Yes.Simplex is the same as phantom power. Many years ago, both names were used. Phantom power has become the preferred term.
