Title: SM57 or SM58 with switch
Id: 1219
Legacy Id: 1263

Question:
Is there a model of the SM57 that has a on and off switch? Or an SM58 with the same switch?


Answer:

	The SM57 is not available with a switch. The model 545SD is very similar to the SM57 and does have a switch. The model SM58S also has a switch.
