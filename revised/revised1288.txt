Title: How do I connect a WL93 to the U1L?
Id: 1288
Legacy Id: 1336

Question:
How should a WL93 be connected to a Lemo for a U1 with the Lemo connector??


Answer:

	Black wire - Pin 1
	Red wire - Pin 2
	Shield - Pin 4
	
	The pin out for the Lemo connector can be found on page 4 of the User Guide Supplement for the UHF Series. You can find the User Guide Supplement at the following link:
	
	Specs on Shure UHF wireless
