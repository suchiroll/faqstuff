Title: What is the difference between the BG 4.1 and the SM94?
Id: 1359
Legacy Id: 1411

Question:
The BG4.1 and SM94 look the same. My question is, which is better when it comes to overhead stereo miking of a drum kit and church choir recording.


Answer:

	The SM94 is slightly quieter (less hiss), but the difference is very small.
