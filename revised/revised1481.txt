Title: N111E for RXT6 cartridge
Id: 1481
Legacy Id: 1534

Question:
I understand that the replacement stylus (N111HE ) for my RXT6 cartridge has been discontinued. What model would you suggest for replacing my cartridge?


Answer:

	As you noted, the N111E stylus is no longer available. Consider the M92E cartridge as a replacement.
