Title: M67 & M68 compared to M267 & M268
Id: 1645
Legacy Id: 1702

Question:
What were the changes from the older models to the newer?


Answer:

	M267 compared to M67:
	- much quieter
	- on-board battery compartment
	- output limiter
	- phantom power
	- mic level and line level switches on inputs and outputs
	- headphone volume control
	- M267 is MUCH better than the M67
	
	M68 compared to M268
	- much quieter
	- phantom power
	- mix bus jack
	- M268 is somewhat better than the M68
