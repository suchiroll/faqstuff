Title: Specs for M91MGD cartridge
Id: 1647
Legacy Id: 1704

Question:
I need the specifications of phonograph cartridge Shure M91MGD that I use in my 1219 Dual.


Answer:

	The M91MGD is a conical (spherical) stylus with a radius of .0006 inches. The tracking force range is 0.75 to 1.5 grams.
