Title: Technical info for EM28A
Id: 1653
Legacy Id: 1710

Question:
THE DESCRIPTIVE PLATE READ:


SHURE BROTHERS, INC.

Controlled magnetic microphone

MODEL #EM28A

Made in EVANSTON, ILL. U.S.A.

U.S. PATENT D-198,850


Answer:

	The EM28A was a custom model for General Electric. Once a custom model is discontinued, all engineering drawings are sent to the company that purchased the mic, in this case, General Electric.
