Question:
The Westone #58 earmold is no longer available and has been replaced by the #56 according to my audiologist.  Can you tell me if the E5 will fit the Westone #56 earmold?


Answer:

	According to Shure Engineering, the Westone model #56 earmold fits the E5 earpiece and the E1 earpiece.
