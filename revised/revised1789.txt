Title: Conference room recording
Id: 1789
Legacy Id: 1849

Question:
We are looking for board room recording system. We would like to use 12 microphone for each member and in to a one unit mixer if possible then to record at the same time a minimum time of our meeting are 2 hours. Do you have any suggestions?


Answer:

	For the microphones, consider the Microflex line.
	
	For the mixer, consider the Shure SCM810.
	
	For a recording device, consider products from Teac or Marantz Professional.
