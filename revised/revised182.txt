Title: FP22 line level inputs
Id: 182
Legacy Id: 183

Question:
In the FP22 headphone amplifier, is the input clipping level for the XLR (line level) 50 V or does that only apply to the Stereo line with the -30 dB pad?

Answer:

	The +34 dBV (50 V) clip level applies to the XLR line position.
