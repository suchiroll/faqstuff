Title: Connector on MX392/0
Id: 1848
Legacy Id: 1908

Question:
Could you please tell me if the connector on the MX392/0 is a mini XLR 3 pin?


Answer:

	The MX392 is supplied without a connector. The cable is permanently connected to the mic; the other end of the cable is bare-ended.
	
	The MX393 is supplied with a mini XLR 3 pin.
