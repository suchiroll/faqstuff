Title: Boundary mic for grand piano
Id: 1883
Legacy Id: 1943

Question:
What boundary mic do you recommend for live sound reinforcement of a 10' grand piano?


Answer:

	The Beta 91A if you require a boundary mic. The SM81 or KSM137 if a boundary mic is not required.
