Title: U4 Receiver: Determining the firmware
Id: 1903
Legacy Id: 1964

Question:
How do I determine the firmware version of the U4?


Answer:

	To determine what version is installed in the U4 receiver.
	
	1. Power on the receiver.
	
	2. Press the + and - buttons for 5 seconds.
	
	3. Firmware version will show in the LCD display.
	
	4. Must be version 2.0 or higher to work properly with the UA888/UA111 software.
