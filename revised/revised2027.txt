Title: Does the SCM810 come with a cable to link to another unit?
Id: 2027
Legacy Id: 2092

Question:
Does the SCM810 come with the connecting cable to link it to another unit? Can you hook a SCM810 up to a SCM410? We only need 10 channels. Also, How do you hook up a SCM810 to and SCM268?


Answer:

	Does the SCM810 come with the connecting cable to link it to another unit? *Yes.
	
	Can you hook a SCM810 up to a SCM410? *Yes.
	
	How do you hook up a SCM810 to and SCM268? *Aux Output of the SCM268 can feed the Aux Input of the SCM810.
