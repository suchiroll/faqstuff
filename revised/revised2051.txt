Title: Connecting a wireless to a guitar amp
Id: 2051
Legacy Id: 2116

Question:
I play Bass guitar and I plan on purchasing a TGS T-Series Wreless system. I was wondering how do you plug the system into your amp.

Thanx


Answer:

	The receiver has a 1/4 output, just like your guitar. Connect a 1/4 to 1/4 cable between the receiver and your amp.
