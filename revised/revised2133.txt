Title: Windscreeen for Shure 512 Microphone
Id: 2133
Legacy Id: 2203

Question:
Does the Shure 512 microphone come with a windscreen built-in to the microphone unit, or is it a separate detachable windscreen?


Answer:

	The 512 is supplied with a detachable foam windscreen. The replacement part number is the RK184WS.
