Title: Reduction of 885TT P.T.T. Delay
Id: 22
Legacy Id: 22

Question:
How can I reduce or remove the P.T.T. delay of the 885TT mic?

Answer:

	To remove the hang time (delay between PTT release and TX drop) youll need to remove D202 (diode) and C202 (capacitor). Replace R205 (resistor) with a jumper.
