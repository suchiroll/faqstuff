Title: XLR connector wiring and mic cable gauge
Id: 2268
Legacy Id: 2349

Question:
What general gauge and wire type do you need to make a short XLR microphone cable extension? If the XLR connector has an additional lug for the connector case, do you jumper that to pin 1 (shield)?


Answer:

	What general gauge and wire type do you need to make a short XLR microphone cable extension?

	* 22 or 24 gauge.
	
	If the XLR connector has an additional lug for the connector case, do you jumper that to pin 1 (shield)?

	* For use with a microphone, yes. For use from electronic gear to electronic gear, no.
