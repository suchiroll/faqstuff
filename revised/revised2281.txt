Title: Replacement for N111HE stylus
Id: 2281
Legacy Id: 2363

Question:
I have a M111HE cartridge. The specified replacement stylus is a N111HE. These are no longer made. Can I use a N111E? Also, what would be a replacement cartridge?


Answer:

	Yes, the N111E (Elliptical) can be used in place of the N111HE (Hyper-Ellipitical).
	
	Replacement cartridge: M97xE
