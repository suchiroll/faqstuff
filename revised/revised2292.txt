Title: Replacement for M75CS.
Id: 2292
Legacy Id: 2377

Question:
I need the cartridge/stylus specifications for a Shure M75CS cartridge.


Answer:

	M75CS

	
	Tracks at 3 to 5 grams.
	
	Spherical styuls with a radius of .0006.
	
	Response: 20 to 20,000 Hz
	
	Replacement stylus is N75B Type 2 that tracks at 1.5 to 3 grams.
