Title: Replacement Stylus for R1000XT
Id: 2324
Legacy Id: 2410

Question:
What is the replacement stylus for the R1000XT cartridge from Radio Shack?


Answer:

	The NE95ED stylus that fits the R1000XT.
	
	Note: There may be other pages in the Shure web site that indicates the N91ED stylus is the replacement. These pages are incorrect; the N91ED does not fit!
