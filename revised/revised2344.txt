Title: Shure Phono Cartridges - Moving Magnet or Moving Coil?
Id: 2344
Legacy Id: 2431

Question:
Are Shure phono cartridges a moving magnet design or moving coil design?


Answer:

	All modern Shure phono cartridges are a moving magnet design. In the 1950s, Shure had the first U.S. patent for the moving magnet phono cartridge.
