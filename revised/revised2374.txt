Title: R27E Cartridge - Replacement?
Id: 2374
Legacy Id: 2465

Question:
What do you have that replaces the above cartridge? Please include info on stylus. Comes from a Realistic Lab 36.


Answer:

	Which phono cartridge will fit my turntable?
	
	
	The R27E uses the N75ED Type 2 replacement stylus. You could replace the stylus in the R27E and the performance of the cartridge will be like new.
