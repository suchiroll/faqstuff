Title: 55SH Series II and Phantom Power?
Id: 2376
Legacy Id: 2467

Question:
Does the 55SH Series II require Phantom Power? If not, what happens if it encounters Phantom Power?



Answer:

	The 55SH is a dynamic microphone. Dynamic microphones do not require phantom power. Any balanced dynamic microphone, such as the 55SH, will not be damaged by phantom power.
