Title: Replacement stylus and / or cartridge for RS5E
Id: 2387
Legacy Id: 2479

Question:
What is the replacement for a Shure RS5E stylus and / or cartridge.  Can I replace the entire cartridge with a new more available style. Thanks


Answer:

	The stylus replacement is the N75B Type 2.
	A replacement cartridge is the M92E.
