Title: Rack mount for an older model T series headset wireless?
Id: 241
Legacy Id: 247

Question:
I just purchased an older T-series wireless mic from ebay. Do you make a rack mount for the base of these units?


Answer:

	Assuming that the receiver is a model T4V or T4G, the rack mount kit is the model WA391. You may also use a rack tray kit such as the Shure model URT.
