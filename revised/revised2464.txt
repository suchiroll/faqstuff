Title: M81MC cartridge specs
Id: 2464
Legacy Id: 2559

Question:
I recently got an unused M81MC phono cartridge but no spec sheet. What is the tracking force for this cartridge? How old is it? Any info or specs?


Answer:

	M81MC: 15 years old.
	
	Original stylus: N75C; Spherical tip (0.6 mils); 3 to 5 tracking range
	
	Available replacement stylus: N75B Type 2; Spherical tip (0.6 mils); 1.5 to 3 tracking range
	
	No more information is available.
