Title: Rackmounting my ULXS4
Id: 2509
Legacy Id: 2605

Question:
I just purchased a ULXS4 receiver and I need to rackmount it. I see there is a single-unit rackmount kit available. Can I front-mount the antennas with this kit as well?


Answer:

	Rack mount it using the UA506 kit. Bring the antennas to the front with the UA600 kit.
