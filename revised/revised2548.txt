Title: DFR11EQ Version 4 All Clear - Factory Default
Id: 2548
Legacy Id: 2646

Question:
How do I return a DFR11EQ-V4 to the factory default setting? Is there a hard reset?


Answer:

	With the unit powered off, hold down the LOCK and CLEAR buttons.
	
	Power the unit on while holding the LOCK and CLEAR buttons for 10 seconds.
