Title: Aluminum case for the KSM27
Id: 2565
Legacy Id: 2663

Question:
Do you offer an aluminum hard case that holds both the KSM27 and it's shockmount?


Answer:

	Sorry, there is no case specifically for the KSM27.
	
	The A32SC aluminum case fits the KSM32 or KSM44. The foam cut-outs will be oversized for the KSM27, but the A32SC will work.
