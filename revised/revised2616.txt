Title: Antique / Vintage Microphone Museum
Id: 2616
Legacy Id: 2718

Question:
Is there an antique / vintage mic museum or collection I can visit or contact?


Answer:

	The Bob Paquette Microphone Museum - located at
	Select Sound Service
	107 E. National Avenue
	Milwaukee WI 53204
	Phone: 414-645-1600Over 1,000 microphones on display, most made before 1950. Mr. Paquette is one of the world's foremost experts on vintage microphones. This is his private collection and is open to the public by appointment.
