Title: A81WS Windscreen and high frequency response
Id: 2639
Legacy Id: 2741

Question:
What effect, if any, does the windscreen have on the frequency response of a high quality microphone? In particular, using the large windscreen, A81WS, on an SM81. Any high frequency rolloff?


Answer:

	Yes, there is roll-off above 10kHz. The response will be down 2 or 3 dB by 20 kHz.
