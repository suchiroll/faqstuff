Title: 1200 HRP phono cartridge
Id: 2648
Legacy Id: 2750

Question:
My current Shure cartridge for my phonograph just had the stylus fall off. Can I get a replacement cartridge? Model 1200 HRP.


Answer:

	The N111E replacement stylus will fit the 1200 HRP body. Or replace the entire unit with a new Shure M97xE.
