Title: Replacement stylus for V15 LT
Id: 2681
Legacy Id: 2785

Question:
What is the replacement stylus?


Answer:

	The original VN45LT was the original stylus. The VN35MR replacement styli will fit the the V15 LT body. It will look a little odd, because the VN35MR stylus is wider than the V15 LT body, but it does fit and should work fine.
