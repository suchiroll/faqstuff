Title: DFR11EQ - What version is it?
Id: 2699
Legacy Id: 2803

Question:
How are I determine the version of the DFR11EQ I own?


Answer:

	Hardware:
	In the U.S., version 3 has a silver faceplate.
	Outside the U.S., version 3 has a black faceplate.
	Version 4 has a black faceplate with no scene select buttons.
	Version 5 has a black faceplace with scene select buttons
	
	Software:
	Under the HELP header, select ABOUT.
	Also, the start-up screen lists the version number.
	
	The easiest method to determine the version of the hardware and software you have is to connect a computer to it and follow the Software instructions above.
