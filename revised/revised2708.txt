Title: MX392 wire color codes
Id: 2708
Legacy Id: 2812

Question:
What are the color codes for the MX392 microphone cable?


Answer:

	AUDIO WIRES
	Red = audio hot / phantom voltage +
	Black = audio cold / phantom voltage +
	Silver = audio shield / phantom voltage -
	
	LOGIC WIRES
	Orange = LED control / +5 Volts DC
	White = Switch out / +5 Volts DC or 0 Volts DC controlled by MX392 front panel PUSH switch
	Green = Logic ground / 0 Volts DC
	
	This information is also on the schematic page of the MX392 User Guide.
