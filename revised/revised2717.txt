Title: Difference between T Series or UT Series and PG Wireless
Id: 2717
Legacy Id: 2821

Question:
1) What is the difference between the T Series wireless and the Performance Gear (PG) VHF Wireless?

2) What is the difference between the UT Series wireless and the Performance Gear (PG) UHF Wireless?


Answer:

	1) The only product difference is the receiver; it features a new exterior design. The transmitters have stayed the same. All FAQ's about the T series also apply to the Performance Gear VHF.
	
	2) The only product difference is the receiver; it features a new exterior design. The transmitters have stayed the same. All FAQ's about the UT series also apply to the Performance Gear UHF.
