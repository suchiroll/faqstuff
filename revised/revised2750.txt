Title: Five pin XLR for a stereo microphone
Id: 2750
Legacy Id: 2856

Question:
What are the pin connections from a stereo mic to a 5 pin XLR?


Answer:

	There is no audio industry standard.
	
	On the Shure VP88, it is:
	
	pin 1 - shield
	pin 2 - left audio +
	pin 3 - left audio -
	pin 4 - right audio +
	pin 5 - right audio -
