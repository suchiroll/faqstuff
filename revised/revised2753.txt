Title: Shure WL condenser mic to a TOA wireless transmitter
Id: 2753
Legacy Id: 2860

Question:
How do I connect a Shure WL183 to a TOA WM-3310 CO1 transmitter?


Answer:

	To connect a Shure WL condenser mic to a TOA WM-3310 CO1 series transmitter:
	
	• Red wire to tip terminal of 3.5 mm phone jack connector
	• Black wire to sleeve terminal of 3.5 mm phone jack connector
	• Shield to sleeve terminal of 3.5 mm phone jack connector
	
	This wiring was confirmed as correct by a Shure customer in Europe.
