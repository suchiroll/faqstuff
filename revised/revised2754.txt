Title: Longer cable for P6HW?
Id: 2754
Legacy Id: 2861

Question:
What is the length of the P6HW cable? Is it possible to get a longer cable?


Answer:

	The PA720 is 10 feet. The cheapest and easiest way to extend it is by adding two standard XLR mic cables. Shure does not offer a longer version of the PA720.
