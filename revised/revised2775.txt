Title: Mic Element 99A86 for Green Bullet Mic
Id: 2775
Legacy Id: 2883

Question:
I have a Shure mic element Model # 99A86. Before I put it into my Green Bullet, I need to know if it is a high or low impedance unit.


Answer:

	The 99A86 is a controlled magnetic, high impedance element.

	At 1,000 Hz, the impedance is 14,000 ohms.
