Title: DPA 4060 mic to Shure transmitter
Id: 2813
Legacy Id: 2923

Question:
How do I connect a DPA 4060 Miniature Mic to a SHURE transmitter?


Answer:

	DPA recommends the use of their adapter DAD6010 when connecting a DPA 4060 to a Shure transmitter. Please contact DPA to purchase the DAD6010
