Title: From FP410E to FP410 configuration - operating voltage
Id: 2827
Legacy Id: 2937

Question:
How do I change the wiring from a FP410E(220v) to a FP410(110v)?


Answer:

	1) Remove jumper X113 from the main printed circuit board.
	
	2) At selector switch S108, solder a jumper wire between holes 1 and 2.
	
	3) At selector switch S108, solder a jumper wire between holes 3 and 4.
	
	4) Replace the 50 mA fuse with a 100 mA slow blow fuse.
	
	5) Replace the external AC power cord with the proper cord for 110 VAC operation.
