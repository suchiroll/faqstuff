Title: How to use a microphone for speaking
Id: 2885
Legacy Id: 3007

Question:
Please provide tips on how to properly use a handheld microphone for speech.


Answer:

	The rules for speech are simple:

	

	1) Hold the mic by the handle, not by the grill.

	

	2) Point the mic at the corner of your mouth. Do not place it under your nose.

	

	3) Talk at your audience, not at the mic.

	

	4) Keep the mic a consistent distance from the corner of your mouth.
