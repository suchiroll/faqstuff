Title: Shure V15IV-G stylus type, tracking force, price
Id: 2909
Legacy Id: 3033

Question:
I have a V15 type IV-G cartridge. Never heard of this one with the "G" designation-just the type 4 HE or MR. What type of stylus is on this one-also need recommended tracking force?


Answer:

	1) G indicated a spherical stylus tip.
	
	2) Tracking force 0.75 to 1.5 grams.
	
	3) In 1979 - $159.00
