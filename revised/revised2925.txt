Title: MRB - Record Bag
Id: 2925
Legacy Id: 3051

Question:
What are the dimensions of the Shure model MRB LP record bag?


Answer:

	Color: Black
	Width: 14 inches
	Height: 18 inches when closed
	Thickness: 2 inches collapsed; 10 inches expanded
	Compartment for LP records: 18 inches x 12 inches x 6 inches
	
	Flap folds over to cover the records
	Carrying handle
	Over-the-shoulder strap
	Two padded shoulder straps with quick slide buckles
	Multiple zipper pockets and open pockets
