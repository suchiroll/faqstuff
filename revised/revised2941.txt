Title: 1/4 wave antenna with the UA834 RF amplifier?
Id: 2941
Legacy Id: 3068

Question:
Is it OK to use a 1/4 wave antenna connected directly to a UA834 amp instead of a 1/2 wave antenna?


Answer:

	The performance of a 1/4 wave antenna mounted directly to a UA834 amplifier could be acceptable. However, a 1/2 wave antenna is strongly recommended.
