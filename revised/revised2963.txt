Title: Stylus for M78S stylus to play 33 1/3 RPM and 45 RPM records
Id: 2963
Legacy Id: 3091

Question:
Is there a stylus that I could use with the M78S cartridge to play a mono LP or 45 RPM?


Answer:

	The SS35C stylus will fit the M78S body. This stylus has a spherical tip with a radius of 0.7 mils. It is suitable for mono LPs and 45 RPM records.
