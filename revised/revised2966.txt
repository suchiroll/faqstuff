Title: BG 5.0 or BG 5.1 Replacement Grill
Id: 2966
Legacy Id: 3094

Question:
What is the part number for a replacement grill?


Answer:

	The Shure part RPM324G will fit the BG 5.0 or BG 5.1. This replacement grill does not have a green stripe like the original grill for the BG 5 series.
