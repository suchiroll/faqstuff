Title: Putting E2 or E3 in round storage case
Id: 2976
Legacy Id: 3104

Question:
How do I put the E2 into the supplied round case?

Answer:

	1) Start by placing the connector end of the cable through one of the slots in the plastic hub.
	
	
	
	2) Wrap the cable around the outside of the plastic hub.
	
	
	
	3) As you near the end of the cable with the earpieces, place the cable in one of the slots with the earpieces in the middle of the hub.
