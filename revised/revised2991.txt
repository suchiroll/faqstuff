Title: Where to get 3 gram Headshell weight?
Id: 2991
Legacy Id: 3119

Question:
Does Shure offer a 3 gram headshell weight for my DJ turntable?


Answer:

	The part number is 53A2167A. This part is also included in hardware package 90WD1371 and package 90WF1371. Call Shure Parts at 800-516-2525 for price and availabilty.
