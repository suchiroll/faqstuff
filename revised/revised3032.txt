Title: Clothing Clip for Earphones
Id: 3032
Legacy Id: 3162

Question:
Does Shure offer a clip to secure earphone cable to my clothing?

Answer:

	Part number 95A8195 is a clothing clip provided with the Shure microphone WH20. It is available from Shure Parts: U.S. toll-free 800-516-2525.
	
	It may be used for the earphone cable, though it is not designed specifically for that product line.
