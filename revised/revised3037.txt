Title: Minimum Operating Voltage for SLX Transmitter
Id: 3037
Legacy Id: 3167

Question:
The SLX transmitter operates on two 1.5 Vdc cells in series. This configuration provides 3 Vdc.
At what voltage level will the SLX transmitter shut off?


Answer:

	While powered up, the transmitter will shut down when the voltage reached 2.0 Vdc.
	
	To power up, the minimum voltage must be 2.3 Vdc.
