Title: Minimum Operating Voltage for PGX Transmitter
Id: 3038
Legacy Id: 3168

Question:
The PGX transmitter operates on two 1.5 Vdc cells in series. This configuration provides 3 Vdc. At what voltage level will the PGX transmitter shut off?


Answer:

	While powered up, the transmitter will shut down when the voltage reached 2.0 Vdc.

	To power up, the minimum voltage must be 2.3 Vdc.
