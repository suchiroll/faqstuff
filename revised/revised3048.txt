Title: Model 522 - Cable Details
Id: 3048
Legacy Id: 3179

Question:
What is the diameter of the cable attached to the Model 522 Microphone? What about the inner conductors?


Answer:

	What is the diameter of the cable attached to the Model 522 Microphone?
	* 0.28 inch (+/- 0.010 tolerance) The cable has a strain relief.

	Four inner conductors: two shielded, two unshielded. Each has a gauge of AWG 20.
