Title: Shure TTR-103 test record
Id: 3083
Legacy Id: 3215

Question:
What is recorded on the TTR-103? When was it discontinued?


Answer:

	The TTR-103 was discontinued circa 1975. It was a 45 rpm recording on a 12 LP disc.
	
	The TTR-103 contained three trackability and distortion tests:
	
	1) High frequency test - 10.8 kHz pulsed with a 270 Hz repetition rate
	
	2) Mid-frequency test - 1000 Hz plus 1500 Hz
	
	3) Low frequency test - 400 Hz plus 4000 Hz
