Title: PSM700 Emission Bandwidth and Channel Spacing
Id: 3091
Legacy Id: 3223

Question:
Please provide emission designator (example 120K00F3E) and channel spacing (example 125KHZ channel spacing) for the PSM700 transmitter. I'm requesting frequencies for use at overseas military installations.


Answer:

	98K0F8E
	
	400 kHz is minimum spacing - only 32 specific frequencies can be tuned.
	
	HF band: 722 - 746 MHz
	H3 band: 524-554 MHz
	L2 band: 632 - 662 MHz
