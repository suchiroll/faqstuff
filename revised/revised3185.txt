Title: Digital Stylus Force Gauge
Id: 3185
Legacy Id: 3325

Question:
Is there a digital alternative to the mechanical Shure SFG-2 stylus force gauge?


Answer:

	Shure does not sell a digital stylus force gauge. Hi-fi enthusiasts have been known to use a digital jewelry scale to set tonearm tracking weight.
	
	To find a source, search the Internet using the keywords digital jewelry scale. Make certain the digital scale has accuracy to 0.1 gram or better.
