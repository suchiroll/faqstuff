Title: MX692/C Wireless Boundary Microphone Discontinued?
Id: 3383
Legacy Id: 3571

Question:
When was the MX692/C wireless boundary microphone discontinued?


Answer:

	The MX692/C was discontinued in November 2007. Remaining inventory is expected to be depleted before January 2008.
	
	The final retail price of the MX692 in the U.S. was $798.00.
