Title: AKG C 577 to Shure wireless bodypack transmitter
Id: 3405
Legacy Id: 3595

Question:
How do I connect the AKG C 577 to a Shure transmitter that has a T4M connector?


Answer:

	Per AKG Technical Support:
	
	TA4F Pin 1 - shield and blue wire
	TA4F Pin 2 - 5,600 ohmresistor, 1/8 watt,to pin 3
	TA4F Pin 3 - red wire
	TA4F Pin 4 - jumper to pin 3
