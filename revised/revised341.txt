Title: Where can I get a windscreen for a MX418?
Id: 341
Legacy Id: 348

Question:
I lost the windscreen for a MX418.  Where can I get another one?

Answer:

	The foam ball windscreen is the A99WS.
	
	The locking metal windscreen is the A412MWS.
	
	The snap fit foam windscreen is the RK412WS. This model contains four windscreens.
	
	Call Shure Parts at 800-516-2525 to obtain these products.
