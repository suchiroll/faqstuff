Title: Using a PGX transmitter with a PG receiver
Id: 3420
Legacy Id: 3611

Question:
Are& PGX transmitters compatible with a PG receiver?


Answer:

	A PG series receiver must be used with a PG series transmitter.   A PG receiver will not sync with a PGX transmitter, nor will a PGX receiver sync with a PG transmitter.
