Title: R25XT output level
Id: 3476
Legacy Id: 3673

Question:
What is the output voltage of the R25XT cartridge?


Answer:

	The R25XT was based on the Shure M75EJ-Type 2.
	
	The output level spec for the M75EJ-Type 2 is 5.0 mV per channel / at 1,000 Hz / at 5 cm/second peak recorded velocity
