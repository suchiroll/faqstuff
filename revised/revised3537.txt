Title: KSM137 KSM141 case dimensions
Id: 3537
Legacy Id: 3741

Question:
What are the dimensions for the plastic case that comes with the KSM137 or KSM141?


Answer:

	The protective carrying case model numbers for the KSM137 and KSM141 microphones are:

	
	Microphone - Carrying Case Model
	KSM137 (single) - A137C
	KSM137 (stereo pair) - A137SPC
	KSM141 (single) - A141C
	KSM141 (stereo pair) - A141SPC

	

	The dimensions are 9-3/8 x 8 x 2-1/8 as indicated in the picture below.
