Title: PSM700 and PSM400 inter-compatibility in X1 band
Id: 3545
Legacy Id: 3751

Question:
Do the specific frequencies that PSM400 and PSM700 operate on in the X1 band align at any point? For example, would it be possible to use a P7R with a P4T?


Answer:

	P4R used with P7T
	
	It also works with a P7R and P4T.
	
	3 shared frequencies in the X1 band:951.875 / 944.125 /944.975 MHz
