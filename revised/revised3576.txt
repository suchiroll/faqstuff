Title: X2u with XBOX 360?
Id: 3576
Legacy Id: 3782

Question:
Will the Shure X2u XLR-to-USB Signal Adapter work with the XBOX 360?


Answer:

	No. The Shure X2U is designed to be used with a  computer that has recording software installed. Basically, the X2U is a substitute for a sound card / mic preamp in a computer.
