Title: Replacement Ear Pads
Id: 3634

Question:
What are the part numbers for replacement ear pads?


Answer:

	HPAEC240: ear pads for SRH240
	HPAEC440: ear pads for SRH440
	HPAEC750: ear pads for SRH750DJ
	HPAEC840: ear pads for SRH840
	[The HPAEC840 pads will also will fit the SRH440.]
	To purchase in the United States, contact Shure Parts at 800-516-2525.
