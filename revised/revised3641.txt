Title: Straight cable for SRH440 and SRH840
Id: 3641
Legacy Id: 3849

Question:
Is there are straight cable for the SRH440 and the SRH840? I would prefer it to the coiled cable.


Answer:

	Yes.  The straight cable is model HPASCA1. It is 2.5 meters (8' 4") in length. The cable can be ordered from Shure Parts at 800-516-2525.
