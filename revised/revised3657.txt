Title: PG Wired Mics: Impedance and Output Level
Id: 3657
Legacy Id: 3865

Question:
What is the impedance and output level of each PG wired microphone model?


Answer:

	PG48: 600 ohms, -52 dBV/Pa (2.5 mV)
	PG52: 300 ohms, -55 dBV/Pa (1.8 mV)
	PG56: 200 ohms, -56 dBV/Pa (1.6 mV)
	PG57: 200 ohms, -56 dBV/Pa (1.6 mV)
	PG58: 300 ohms, -53 dBV/Pa (2.2 mV)
	PG81: 600 ohms, -48 dBV/Pa (4.0 mV)
