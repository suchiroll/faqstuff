Title: FP42 discontinuation
Id: 3686
Legacy Id: 3895

Question:
When was the Shure FP42 stereo mixer discontinued?


Answer:

	The FP42 was discontinued in 2004.  The final U.S. retail price was $1,315.  In some cases, the Shure FP33 mixer may be used in place of the FP42.  Also, mixers similar to the FP42 are maufactured by Sound Devices.
