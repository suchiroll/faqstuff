Title: RK100PK Dimensions And Data
Id: 3689
Legacy Id: 3903

Question:
What are the dimensions of the RK100PK in-line preamp and general electronic specs?


Answer:

	Length: 4 inches
	Diameter: 3/4 inch
	Input: 4 pin male TA4M connector
	Output: 3 pin male XLR connector
	Color: black
	Phantom Power Requirements:  12 to 48 Vdc
	Gain from Input to Output: 12dB
	Gain Modification: 0dB can be obtained by changing four surface mount resistors.  This modification is difficult and is recommended only for experienced electronic technicians.
