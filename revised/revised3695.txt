Title: P9T Transmitter Audio Level LED Meter
Id: 3695
Legacy Id: 3912

Question:
The P9T transmitter has two strings of LEDs that act as an audio level meter for the Left Input and for the Right Input. How do I interpret these LEDs?


Answer:

	With the P9T set to: Line Input +4dBu; Input Level-16dB, the chart provides the corresponding signal levels.

	
		
			
				
					
						LED #
				
				
					
						Input Level
				
			
			
				
					
						8 (Red)/INPUT CLIP
				
				
					
						+20.4 dBV  1 dBV
				
			
			
				
					
						7 (Yellow)
				
				
					
						+18.4 dBV  1 dBV
				
			
			
				
					
						6 (Yellow)
				
				
					
						+14.4 dBV  1 dBV
				
			
			
				
					
						5 (Green)
				
				
					
						+9.4 dBV  1 dBV
				
			
			
				
					
						4 (Green)
				
				
					
						+2.9 dBV  1 dBV
				
			
			
				
					
						3 (Green)
				
				
					
						-2.6 dBV  1 dBV
				
			
			
				
					
						2 (Green)
				
				
					
						-7.6 dBV  1 dBV
				
			
			
				
					
						1 (Green)
				
				
					
						-12.6 dBV  1 dBV
