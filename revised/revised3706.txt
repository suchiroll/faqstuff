Title: KSM44/SL Discontinuation
Id: 3706
Legacy Id: 3934

Question:
When was the KSM44/SL discontinued?


Answer:

	The KSM44/SL was discontinued in July 2010.  It was replaced by the KSM44A. The final U.S. retail price of the KSM44/SL was $999.00.
	These KSM44/SL accessories were also discontinued:
	A44SC - aluminum carrying case ($80 U.S.)
	A44VB - storage pouch ($13 U.S.)
	A44SM - shock mount ($142 U.S.)
	A44M - swivel mount ($66 U.S.)
