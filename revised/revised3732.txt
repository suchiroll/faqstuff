Title: Error installing Wireless Workbench 5.0.3 on Windows 7
Id: 3732
Legacy Id: 3966

Question:
I am trying to install Wireless Workbench version 5.0.3 on a computer with Windows 7 and I get an error message saying that this is not a valid application, would you like to reinstall.



Answer:

	Simply click on reinstall and the Wireless Workbench will install.  Do not click on Cancel.
