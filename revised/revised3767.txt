Title: PG27 Stand Adapter
Id: 3767
Legacy Id: 4003

Question:
How do I purchase a replacement mic stand adapter for the PG27?


Answer:

	As of September 2010, this part may be purchased from Shure Parts (800-516-2525) for $17.50.
	
	The part number is 95A15045. The stand adapter will also work with the PG42-XLR and PG42-USB.
