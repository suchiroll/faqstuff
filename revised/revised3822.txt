Title: SRH940 and SRH840 Comparison
Id: 3822
Legacy Id: 4060

Question:
Please explain the primary feature differences between the SRH940 and the SRH840 headphones.


Answer:

	
		SRH940 is supplied with a hard plastic carrying case, a coiled cable, and a straight cable.
	
		SRH940 has a flatter frequency response and lower distortion.
	
		SRH940 has an improved headband design that is more comfortable to wear and better balanced on the head.
	
		The SRH940 has ear cups that swivel 90 degrees.
	
		The SRH940 has velour ear pads that provide better comfort than leatherette ear pads.
