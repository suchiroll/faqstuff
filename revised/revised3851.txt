Title: Temperature limits of AXT900 charger
Id: 3851
Legacy Id: 4091

Question:
What are the temperature limits of the AXT900 charger?


Answer:

	The AXT900 will charge the batteries if the temperature is between 0 Centigrade (32F)and 40 Centigrade (104F). If the temperature is below or above these limits, it will not charge any batteries.
