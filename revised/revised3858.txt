Title: RP325 Cable
Id: 3858
Legacy Id: 4100

Question:
Please provide details on the RP325 cable.


Answer:

	The cable is 10 feet in length. There are two inner conductors, twisted, with a shield.

	One end is a female XLR connector. The other end is a male, 3.5mm phone plug.

	

	Wired as follows:

	XLR pin 1 - cable shield - phone plug sleeve

	XLR pin 2 - red inner conductor - phone plug sleeve

	XLR pin 3 - black inner conductor - phone plug tip

	Phone plug ring is not connected.
