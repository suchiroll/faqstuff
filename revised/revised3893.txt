Title: PSM 1000 Frequency Bands in the U.S.
Id: 3893
Legacy Id: 4139

Question:
What frequency bands are offered in the U.S. for the PSM 1000?


Answer:

	G10: 470 to 542 MHz (TV 14 - TV 25)

	

	J8: 554 to 626 MHz (TV 28 - TV 39) Note: For J8, TV 37 is omitted (608 to 614 MHz)

	

	L8:626 to698 MHz (TV40 - TV 51)
