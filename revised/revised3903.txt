Title: UHF-R transmitter with AXT400 receiver?
Id: 3903
Legacy Id: 4149

Question:
Can the UHF-R transmitters, such as the UR1 or UR2 work with the Axient AXT400 receiver?



Answer:

	Yes, the UHF-R transmitters will work with the AXT400 receiver. However, certain features of Axient, such as Showlink, will not work with the UHF-R transmitters.
