Title: SM89 Discontinuation and Replacement
Id: 3911
Legacy Id: 4157

Question:
When was the SM89 discontinued? What was the final price? What replaced the SM89?


Answer:

	The SM89 shotgun mic was introduced in 1987 with a U.S. retail price of $900. It was discontinued in August 2011 with a U.S. retail price of $932.
	
	The SM89 was replaced by the VP89L.
