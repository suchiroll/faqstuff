Title: Internal wiring of the Beta 58A XLR connector
Id: 4
Legacy Id: 4

Question:

Beta 58A, XLR connector, pulled out came off. What is the correct internal wiring of the Beta 58A XLR connector?


Answer:

	The Beta 58A XLR connector should be wired as follows:
	
	Pin 1 -- Shield/Chassis
	Pin 2 -- Blue
	Pin 3 -- Yellow
	
	The pin numbers should be displayed in the connector itself.
