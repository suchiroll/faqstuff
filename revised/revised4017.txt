Title: Mic element 90A2263
Id: 4017
Legacy Id: 4424

Question:
Would you be able to send specs on your microphone element 90A2263?


Answer:

	This element is also called the R132 and is used in the model 527C.It is a dynamic mic element.
	
	Nominal impedance at 1 kHz:150 ohms
	
	Nominal output at 94 dB SPL (One Pascal) at 1 kHz: -51 dBV (2.8 mV)
	
	Specified frequency response range: 300 Hz to 5,000 Hz
