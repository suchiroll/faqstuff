Title: Preamp RK100PK for Shure WB98H/C?
Id: 4037
Legacy Id: 4469

Question:
Will the RK100PK Preamp work with the Shure WB98H/C mic?


Answer:

	The RPM626 is the preferred preamp for changing the WB98H/Cto a wired application. The RK100PK is not recommended as it will be somewhat noisy and possibly distorted when used for miking an instrument.
