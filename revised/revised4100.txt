Title: RPM626 improved headroom
Id: 4100
Legacy Id: 4735

Question:
When was the performance of the RPM626 improved? What changed? How is the improved RPM626 identified?


Answer:

	In the spring of 2012, a running change was made to the RPM626 inline preamp - the headroom was increased by 6 dB. The improved preamp design can be identified by a blue dot between the words SHURE and BETA printed on the RPM626 exterior.
