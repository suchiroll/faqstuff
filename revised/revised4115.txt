Title: VNSE5MR stylus vs. VN5MR stylus
Id: 4115
Legacy Id: 4897

Question:
What is the difference between a Shure VNSE5MR stylus and a Shure VN5MR?Which one was produced first? Is there a difference in sound between thetwo?


Answer:

	VNSE5MR was designed to fit the VST V - a Special Edition cartridge.
	
	VN5MR was designed to fit the V15V-MR.It was produced first.
	
	The styli can be interchanged.
	
	
	We find nothing in the engineering specs to indicate that one stylus was better than the other.
