Title: UA870USTV Discontinuation - Directional Antenna with RF amplifier
Id: 4118
Legacy Id: 4900

Question:
When was the UA870USTV discontinued? What was the final price? What replaced it?


Answer:

	The UA870USTV was discontinued May 2012. The final U.S. retail price was $299. It was replaced by the UA874US.
