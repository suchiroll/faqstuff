Title: SRH940 velour earpads - how to clean
Id: 4131
Legacy Id: 5014

Question:
My SRH940 headphones come with a replacement set of velour ear pads. Can the original and the replacement sets be washed and if so how?


Answer:

	Wash gently by hand.Use warm water with a small amount of Dawn liquid soap.Rinse well in clear water. Pat dry with towel.Let air dry for 24 hours at minimum.
