Title: 550L Discontinuation and Suggested Alternative
Id: 4143
Legacy Id: 5045

Question:
When was the model 550L discontinued? What is a suggested replacement?


Answer:

	The model 550L was discontinued in 2008. Remaining stock was depleted by 2012. The final U.S. retail price was $112.

	

	Recommended replacement is the model 450 Series II.
