Title: SRH240M+ Color Code of Cable Wires
Id: 4148
Legacy Id: 5054

Question:
What is the color code for the wires inside of the SRH240M+ cable?


Answer:

	Blue: Left Ear Phone +
	Gray: Left Ear Phone -
	Red: Right Ear Phone +
	Black: Right Ear Phone -
	Yellow: Microphone +
	Shield: Microphone -
