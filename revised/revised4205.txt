Title: SE215LTD-EFS
Id: 4205
Legacy Id: 5172

Question:
Please provide details about the special edition of the SE215 - the SE215LTD-EFS.


Answer:

	Introduced in the autumn of 2012, the SE215LTD is variation of the standard SE215.
	
	SE215LTD differences
	Case color: Blue
	Cable length: 46 inches
	Sound signature: Slightly warmer, minor boost in the low frequency response.
	Accessories: same as SE215
	
	Model number for the United States: SE215LTD-EFS
	Model numbers for Asia: SE215SPE-A; SE215SPE-CHN
