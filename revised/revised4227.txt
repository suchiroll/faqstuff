Title: SCM820 Crestron/AMX Control Strings
Id: 4227
Legacy Id: 5211

Question:
I want to control the SCM820 with a Crestron touchscreen. What are the control strings?


Answer:

	The SCM820 can be controlled by AMX, Crestron, Extron, etc.
	
	The touch screen can control/monitor:

	
		Volume Controls
	
		Mute
	
		Metering
	
		Channel and Device Names
	
		Clip Indicators
	
		Gate Indicators
	
		Force Gating
	
		More ...
