Title: WA580 Cloth Pouch
Id: 4271
Legacy Id: 5334

Question:
What devices fit into the WA580 cloth pouch?


Answer:

	UR1 transmitter
	ULXD1 transmitter
	P9R receiver
	
	There is a hole in the pouch for the antenna to protrude.
	Approximate dimensions for the pouch (madewitha stretchable material): 4 H x 2 W x 0.75 D
	
	There are two belts that come with the WA580 bodypack pouch. One is 20 inches long and one is 30 inches long.They can be velcroed together to form an extra-large single belt.
