Title: BLX1 Bodypack Transmitter - Feature Changes
Id: 4291
Legacy Id: 5360

Question:
How has the BLX1 transmitter been improved compared to the SLX1 or PGX1 transmitter?


Answer:

	For the BLX1 transmitter:
	
	Increased battery life: up to 14 hours of operation from two AA cells
	
	On/Off toggle switch: tactile indication that the switch has been activated
	
	Continuously variable audio input gain control: range of 26 dB
	
	Bi-color LED battery status indicator: Green (battery OK) or Red (less than 60 minutes of operating time)
