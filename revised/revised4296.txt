Title: BLX - number of individual frequencies
Id: 4296
Legacy Id: 5365

Question:
For a BLX systems sold in the United States, how many individual frequenices are programmed into a receiver?


Answer:

	The quantity is 123. Note that certain frequencies may be re-used in different groups. For example, Group B Channel 1 might be the same exact frequency at Group D Channel 0.

	Under ideal conditions, the maximum theoretical compatible systems available within one frequency group are 12.
