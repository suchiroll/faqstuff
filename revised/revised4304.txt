Title: AXT100 or AXT200 with FP5 or UR5?
Id: 4304
Legacy Id: 5375

Question:
Will the AXT 100 and AXT 200 work with the FP5 or UR5?


Answer:

	1) Will not work with the FP5.
	
	2) Will work with the UR5, assuming the frequency bands overlap. Tone key squelch may need to be disabled on the UR5. See the UR5 User Guide.
