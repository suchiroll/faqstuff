Title: SM48W-LC
Id: 4317
Legacy Id: 5391

Question:
What is the model SM48W-LC?


Answer:

	The SM48W-LC is a Shure model SM48, without a cable, that has a white handle and a white label. The audio specifications are the same as a standard SM48.
	
	The SM48W-LC is sold only in Guitar Center storesbeginning in August 2013. This is a special edition microphone and will be sold only for a limited time period.
