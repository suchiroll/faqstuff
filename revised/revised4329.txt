Title: WA306 Cable versus WA302 Cable
Id: 4329
Legacy Id: 5411

Question:
How is the WA306 cable different than the WA302 cable?



Answer:

	The WA306 has a gold-plated Neutrik 1/4 phone plug connector.The WA306 cable has lower handling noise and is more durable. The WA306 TQG connectoris better quality; it has a push-button lock.
	
	The WA306 does not replace the WA302. Both cables are still offered as of October 2013.
