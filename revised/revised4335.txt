Title: Thread Diameter on A89M-CC, A89M-PG, A89LM-BA
Id: 4335
Legacy Id: 5418

Question:
What is the female thread diameter on the A89M-CC, A89M-PG, and A89LM-BA?


Answer:

	The female thread diameter is 3/8 - a standard used on boom poles. Included with each of these mounts is an adapter: 5/8-27 female thread to 3/8 male thread.
