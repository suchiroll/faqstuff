Title: Installing A2WS on the SM57
Id: 4376
Legacy Id: 5467

Question:
Is the set screw supposed to fit in the groove just below the mic head or is it supposed to tighten against the plastic shim supplied with the A2WS?



Answer:

	
		Tighten against the plastic shim.
	
		Leave an air space, as much as feasible, between the SM57 grill and the A2WS foam.
