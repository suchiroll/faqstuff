Title: Model CM9L
Id: 4382
Legacy Id: 5474

Question:
What information is there about the model CM9L?



Answer:

	
		CM9L is not a Shure model number. It is a model number from another manufacturer who used a Shure mic as part of their radio equipment. This is called an OEM model.
	
		The model CM9L is the same as the Shure model 401B.
	
		Information about OEM mics.
