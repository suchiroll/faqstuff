Title: Gold Shure Microphones
Id: 4396

Question:
Did Shure ever sell gold microphones that were available to the public?


Answer:

	Yes. Here is a list from a 1969 Shure catalog (#AL314D).
	
	55S Gold - $97
	55SW Gold - $99
	545S Gold - $104
	545 Gold - $99
	545L Gold - $84
	550S Gold - $82
	565 Gold - $110
	585SA Gold - $78
