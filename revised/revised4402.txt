Title: GLX-D Interchangable Mic Elements?
Id: 4402
Legacy Id: 5498

Question:
Does the GLXD24 microphone system have the option of interchangeable capsules? Can someone switch a SM58 capsule with a SM86 capsule?



Answer:

	Sorry, the answer is no. The mic element on the GLX-D handheld transmitter is fixed.
