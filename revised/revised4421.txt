Title: Shorter Cable for SE Series Earphones
Id: 4421
Legacy Id: 5518

Question:
I have SE model earphones with the detachable cable. Is there a short cable available?


Answer:

	As of January 2014, there is a shorter cable available from the Shure Parts: 800-516-2525.It is a 46 inch long,gray cable available for $17.96 (January 2014 price.) The part number is 95G9233.
