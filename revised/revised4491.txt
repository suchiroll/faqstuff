Title: 596SK Microphone
Id: 4491
Legacy Id: 5605

Question:
Did Shure make a model 596SK?


Answer:

	Yes. It was an OEM model and was sold for the Silent Knight Intelligent Fire Alarm Control Systems. The mic cable terminated in an RJ-45 plug.
	
	Like all OEM models, once discontinued, the microphone technical documentation was sent to the purchaser, Silent Knight.
