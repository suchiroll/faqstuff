Title: Mount SLX4 and SCM268 side by side?
Id: 4534
Legacy Id: 5653

Question:
Can I rack mount an SLX4 receiver and an SCM268 side-by-side in the same rack space? Can I use the included rack-mount hardware?



Answer:

	If both units are purchased new, you will have all the required hardware to mount the units, side by side, in one rack space.
