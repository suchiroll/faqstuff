Title: ULXP Discontinuation
Id: 4538
Legacy Id: 5657

Question:
When were the ULXP4 receiver and all ULXP systems discontinued?


Answer:

	August 2014. The ULXS4 receiver and the ULXS systems were NOT discontinued
	
	The final U.S. retail price for the ULXP4 receiver was $770. It can be replaced by the ULXS4 receiver for most applications.
	
	The ULXP4 was introduced in 2002.
