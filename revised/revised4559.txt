Title: BLX Transmitter and 1.5V Lithium Cell
Id: 4559
Legacy Id: 5683

Question:
I have a BLX2 transmitter. When I use an Energizer non-rechargeable lithium 1.5V battery / cell, the red LED flashes. Guidance, please.

Answer:

	This issue occurred with the early BLX transmitters. It has been resolved with a firmware revision.
	
	Shure Service will do a firmware update that will fix this issue.Please contact Shure Service at 800-516-2525 for instructions on returning the BLX transmitter for the firmware change.
