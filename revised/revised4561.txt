Title: QLX-D transmitter - NiMH AA battery recommendation
Id: 4561
Legacy Id: 5686

Question:
Will the QLXD2 work with standard NiMH AA batteries?

Answer:

	The battery fuel gauge on the QLX-D transmitters is optimized for the SB900A rechargeable battery packor AA alkaline cells.It is OK to use NiMH cells but the battery fuel gauge may not be accurate.
