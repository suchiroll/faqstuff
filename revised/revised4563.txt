Title: Me75P Wiring
Id: 4563
Legacy Id: 5688

Question:
What is the function of each pin at the rear of the Me75P cartridge?

Answer:

	Looking at the rear of this P mount cartridge:
	
	Upper pin left = right channel positive.Upper pin right = left channel positive
	
	Lower pin left = right channel negative/ground.Lower pin right = left channel negative/ground.
	
	Use bright illumination to see the markings molded into the black plastic.
	
	Color code for phono cartridge wiring
