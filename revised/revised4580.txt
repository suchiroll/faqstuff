Question:
What are sonic differences between the different Unidyne models?



Answer:

	Beloware two audio WAV files that compare the sonic quality of five Unidyne microphones:
	
	Model 5575LE - introduced 2014
	Model Super 55 - introduced 2009
	Model 55SH Series II - introduced 1989
	Model 55S - introduced 1951
	Model 55 - introduced 1939
	
	One WAV file starts starts with the 55 and progresses forward to the 5575LE. The other WAV files with the 5575LE and progresses back to the 55.
