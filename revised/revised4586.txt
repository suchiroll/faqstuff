Title: P9HW, P3R, P3RA Headphone Output Power
Id: 4586
Legacy Id: 5739

Question:
What is the headphone output power for these devices?


Answer:

	Headphone Output Power (both channels driven): 80mW/channel @ 32 ohms.
	
	Headphone Output Power (single channel driven): 120mW @ 32 ohms.
	
	Headphone Output Impedance: 2.5ohms
	
	Designed to drive earphones having an impedance of 2.5 ohms or greater.
