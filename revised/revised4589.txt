Title: EAC9BK Earphone Extension Cable - Dimensions
Id: 4589
Legacy Id: 5744

Question:
Please provide dimensions of the EAC9BK cable.

Answer:

	EAC: Earphone Accessory Cable
	
	9: Total cable length in inches (23 cm)
	
	BK: Black cable
	
	3.5 mm male stereo phone plug: It has four sections.
	1) Tip-Ring-Sleeve metal mating section 15 mm in length
	2) Next is circular plastic section - 3 mm in length and 5.8 mm in diameter
	3) Next is circular plastic section - 13 mm in length and 7.5 mm in diameter
	4) Next is the flexible cable strain relief - 18 mm in length
	
	3.5 mm female stereo phone jack: 35 mm in length including cable strain relief
