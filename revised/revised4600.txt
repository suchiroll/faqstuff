Title: ULXD2 Battery Door Part
Id: 4600
Legacy Id: 5756

Question:
On the ULXD2 plastic battery door, this is small, rectangular part that snaps into the battery side of the door. This part is perpendicular to the main door, and has a rubber pad that presses downward on the AA cells. What is the part number?


Answer:

	Shure part #95A15842. Description: AA Battery Adapter for Shure ULXD2 handheld transmitter.
