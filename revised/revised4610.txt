Title: P9T Rack Mount Hardware
Id: 4610
Legacy Id: 5766

Question:
Is there a rack mount kit for the P9T transmitter?

Answer:

	RPW503 - Single rack mount kit
	
	RPW504 - Dual rack mount kit (two P9T units in one rack space)
	
	Available from Shure Parts and Service.
	
	The P9T has the same chassis as these other Shure units:
	BLX4R
	P3T
	PA411
	SLX4
	ULXD4 (Single receiver only)
