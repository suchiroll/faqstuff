Title: A25E Gray and A25E Black
Id: 4620
Legacy Id: 5779

Question:
When was the A25E changed from gray to black?


Answer:

	This change occurred in March 2015.The model number did not change.
	
	The A25E was originally gray plastic with a brass-color metal threaded insert at the bottom.
	
	The new A25E is black plastic with a silver-colormetal threaded insertat the bottom.
	
	The plastic material used is more flexible than the material used in the A25D. The A25E is typically used for KSM handheld wired mics.
