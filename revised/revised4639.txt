Title: PGA Condenser Mics Self-Noise Specifications
Id: 4639
Legacy Id: 5798

Question:
Please provide the SPL equivalent self-noise ratings for the PGA condenser mics.

Answer:

	PGA27: 15dB SPLtypical, A-weighted
	
	PGA81: 20dB SPLtypical, A-weighted
	
	PGA98D: 27dB SPLtypical, A-weighted
	
	PGA98H: 27dB SPLtypical, A-weighted
	
	PGA181: 22dB SPLtypical, A-weighted
