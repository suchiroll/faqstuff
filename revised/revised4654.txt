Title: SB900A battery used with SBRC Rack Recharger
Id: 4654
Legacy Id: 5817

Question:
Can the SB900A battery be recharged using the SBRC or AXT900US rack mounted recharger?

Answer:

	Yes.Required isthe SBRC rack charging station frame, and the SBC-AX charging module for the SB900A. The SBC-AX module holds two SB900A batteries. Up to four SBC-AX modules fit into the SBRC.
