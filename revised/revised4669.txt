Title: Brass Screw for Desk Stands
Id: 4669
Legacy Id: 5836

Question:
For decades, Shure has used a short, fat, brass fastener to hold a mic, or a mic clip, to a desk stand. This screw / bolt has been used with the S36 stand, S37A stand, S39 stand, and others. What is the Shure part number?


Answer:

	Part # 31C162
	
	External thread: 5/8-27
	
	Thread length: 3/8
	
	Total length of screw: 1/2
	
	Flat screwdriver slot for tightening into the microphone.
