Title: BLX Transmitter Current Draw
Id: 4672
Legacy Id: 5839

Question:
What is the current draw (drain) for a BLX transmitter?


Answer:

	BLX1 and BLX2
	
	Current draw=110mA +/- 15mA @ 3V; with alpha-numeric LEDdisplay (in battery compartment) being dark.
	
	Up to 14 hours of operation from two AA alkaline cells
