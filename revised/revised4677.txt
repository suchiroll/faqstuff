Title: "SPECIAL" SM57 from Randall May International
Id: 4677
Legacy Id: 5844

Question:
Please provide details on Randall May International.


Answer:

	In the 1970s, Randall (Randy) May first purchased standard SM57 mic elements from Shure. He attached the SM57 mic elements to his own custom manufactured handles/housingsfor mounting within the shell of a drum - kick drum, snare drum, tom-tom, etc.
