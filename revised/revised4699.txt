Title: Capacitive specification of GLXD1 transmitter
Id: 4699
Legacy Id: 5869

Question:
The GLX-D1 User Guide lists the input impedance as 900k ohms, but what is the capacitive spec on the input?



Answer:

	It is 900k in parallel with 330pF. If used, the Shure guitar cable adds another 140pF.
