Title: Stylus Terms and Definitions
Id: 4704
Legacy Id: 5874

Question:
What is a nude stylus tip? A bonded stylus tip? A nude mount? A bushing mount?


Answer:

	Nude stylus tip = the entire stylus tip is the same material, typically diamond.
	
	Bonded stylus tip = the stylus tip consists oftwo parts bonded (adhered) together. Thelower portionis diamond and the upper portion is metal or another type of crystal/stone.
	
	Nude mount = the stylus tip is glued directly into a hole at the end of the stylus shank.
	
	Bushing mount = the stylus tip is glued into a bushing affixed to the stylus shank
