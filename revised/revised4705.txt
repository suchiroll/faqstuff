Title: PA411 Fan Noise
Id: 4705
Legacy Id: 5875

Question:
Is there a fan inside of the PA411? At times, I hear a mechanical noise and it could be a fan in operation.



Answer:

	Yes, the PA411 has two internal fans.The fansoperate based on the level of heat inside of the unit. It is normal to hear a mechanical noise when the PA411 is being cooled by the fans. A single fan has a noise rating of 16.0 dBA SPL at one meter.
