Title: Stylus for M22 phono cartridge
Id: 471
Legacy Id: 481

Question:
Can you supply or give me any info on a stylus N22D or equal for an old M22 cartridge?


Answer:

	Sorry, but the N22D stylus has been discontinued for over 25 years.
	
	The M22 phono cartridge tracked at .75 to 2.5 grams and had a spherical stylus with a radius of 0.5 mils.
