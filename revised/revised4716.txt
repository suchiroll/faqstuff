Title: How to export or share recordings from the Motiv app in iTunes
Id: 4716
Legacy Id: 5886

Question:
How can I export or share recordings from the Motiv app in iTunes?


Answer:

	Connect your device to the computer and open iTunes.  
	Click on the iPhone icon, and select "File Sharing" from the column on the left.


Once you highlight the Motiv app, you should see a list of recordings in the right column.


From here, select one or more files and click the  "Save To" button and select a destination folder when you want to copy your files.
