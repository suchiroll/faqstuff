Title: MAC address on an QLX-D
Id: 4725
Legacy Id: 5896

Question:
How do I find the MAC address of a QLX-D microphone receiver?



Answer:

	The MAC address can be viewed by using Shure Wireless Work Bench (WWB) software. The followingsteps assume WWB isinstalled on the computer.
	
	1.Connect the QLX-D receiver to your computer with a standard Ethernet cable. The receiver should automatically appear in the Inventory window.
	2.Right-click on the desired receiver and select Properties
	3.Click the arrow next to the word Settings to reveal more options.
	4.Click the Network tab. The MAC address will be displayed in the lower right-hand corner.
