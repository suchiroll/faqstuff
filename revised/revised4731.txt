Title: How to export or share recordings from the Motiv app
Id: 4731
Legacy Id: 5903

Question:
How can I export or share recordings from the Motiv App?


Answer:

	In the Motiv App, open the MENU and select Recordings.
	
	
	
	Select the EXPORT icon for the recording that you want to export.
	
	
	
	Select the method (email, dropbox, messaging, save as, etc) that you want to export.
