Title: Windows 10 with Shure Wireless Work Bench 6.11 (WWB)
Id: 4734
Legacy Id: 5906

Question:
Is Shure WWB 6.11 compatible with Windows 10?


Answer:

	As of September 1, 2015: While WWB 6.11.2 does not officially support Windows 10,Shures initial tests have shown no defects.Pleaseinform Shure using the WWB6 Forum if issues are found or strange behaviors are observed.
