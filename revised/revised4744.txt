Title: MX392BE Boundary Microphone with Bottom-Exit Cable
Id: 4744
Legacy Id: 5924

Question:
When was the MX392BE introduced?


Answer:

	October 2015.
	
	The MX392BE is supplied with a bottom-exit cable configuration. It also has adhesive pads onits bottom platethat secure the microphone to the table surface. The technical functions of the MX392BEare identical to the standard MX392 model.
