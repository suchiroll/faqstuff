Title: Tuner on GLXD6 Receiver - how to use
Id: 4759
Legacy Id: 5941

Question:
Please provide hints on using the GLX-D6 tuner.



Answer:

	1. Tune the guitar: to do so stomp on the silver colored big metal switch on the GLXD-6.
	
	2. To change parameters of the tuner: first, be in tuner mode (see above) and have the guitar tuner feature working; second, press MODE to toggle through the tuner options (strobe tuning, reference pitch, et al) and use the UP/DOWNarrows to select whatoption is desired.
