Title: QLX-D X52 Band in U.S.
Id: 4761
Legacy Id: 5943

Question:
What is the theoretical maximum number of QLX-D X52 systems that can operate in one venue?


Answer:

	In the X52 band (902.450 to 927.550 MHz), it is feasible to operate up to 12 systems, assuming no local RF interference in this band.
