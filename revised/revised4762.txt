Title: SBC200-US Battery Charger power supply
Id: 4762
Legacy Id: 5944

Question:
What power supply is provided with the SBC200-US?


Answer:

	The model SBC200-US includes a PS45 power supply. Up to three additional SBC200 battery chargers can be daisy-chained to the SBC200-US.

	Therecould be additional letters after PS45 depending upon the country.

	The SBC200 requires a power supply with an output of15 Vdc and 3.3 amps. The SBC200 charging current flow is 0.75 amps (750 milliamps.)
