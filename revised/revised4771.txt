Title: KSE1500 Diaphragm Mass
Id: 4771
Legacy Id: 5954

Question:
I heard that the KSE1500 diaphragm is "virtually mass-less." How much does it weigh?


Answer:

	It takes just over 6,000 KSE1500 diaphragms to equal one gram.
	
	Or it takes 15,000 KSE1500 diaphragms to equal the weight of one U.S. penny (one cent coin).
