Title: Model E98M Phono Cartridge
Id: 4779
Legacy Id: 5963

Question:
What is the replacement stylus for the model E98M?



Answer:

	1) E98M is not a standard Shure model number.
	
	2) E98M was an OEM model number. Read: Shure OEM Products: 1940s to 1990s
	
	3) The replacement stylus/needle for E98M was NDC-HE.Shure has not made or sold the NDC-HE for over 20 years.
	
	4) Please see the following link for sources where you can purchase discontinued Shure styli.You may find the NDC-HE at one of these sources:
	Sources for Discontinued Styli and Phono Cartridges
