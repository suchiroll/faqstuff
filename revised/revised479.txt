Title: V15 type III cartridge loading
Id: 479
Legacy Id: 489

Question:
What is the Cartridge loading capacitance, and loading resistance for the V15 type III?


Answer:

	Optimal load per channel is 47,000 ohms in parallel with 400 to 500 picofarads. Load resistance can be as high as 70,000 ohms with less than 1 dB of change in the frequency response.
