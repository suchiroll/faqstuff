Title: Motiv MV88 signal heard via earphone jack on iPhone
Id: 4791
Legacy Id: 5976

Question:
If I have the MV88 plugged into my phone, can I listen to the signal with the earphone jack on the phone?



Answer:

	Yes.  You will need to turn on "Input Monitor" on the Motiv App. In the menu, select the INFO menu. Turn on "Input Monitor."
