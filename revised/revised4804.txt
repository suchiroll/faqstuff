Title: SE846 Weight
Id: 4804
Legacy Id: 5990

Question:
Please provide the weight specifications for the SE846.


Answer:

	Each SE846 earbud weighs 5.58 grams - measured without the flexible earphone sleeve.
	
	The SE846 cable weighs 25.50 grams.
	
	Thus, the total weight is 36.66 grams.
