Title: Motiv MV5 Mounted to a Mic Stand
Id: 4806
Legacy Id: 5992

Question:
How can I mount the MV5 to a mic stand that has a 5/8"-27 Male thread?


Answer:

	Remove the MV5 from its supplied desk stand.The thread of the MV5 is 1/4-20Female.
	
	Obtain a mechanical adapter that is 5/8-27 Female threadto 1/4-20 Male thread. One such adapter is offered by On Stage Stands, part #MA125. The adapter might be called a camera mount adapter for mic stands.
	
	Mount the adapter to the mic stand and fasten the MV5 to themale thread of the adapter.
