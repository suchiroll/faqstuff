Title: OEM microphone model DM53 or DM57
Id: 4816
Legacy Id: 6002

Question:
Please provide details about the model DM53 made for DeMambro Sound Equipment Company of Boston, Massachusetts. Attached is a photo.


Answer:

	The DM 53 is an OEM variation of the Shure model 530: Model 530 User Guide
	
	DM = Dynamic Microphone or DeMambro
	53 = shortened version of 530
	
	There was also the model DM57 - closely related to the model DM53. The DM57 was based on the Shure model 535.
	
	Both DM models were from the 1950s/1960s.
