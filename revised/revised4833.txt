Title: How to purchase Feature License for DIS-CCU
Id: 4833
Legacy Id: 6019

Question:
How do I purchase a Feature License for the DIS-CCU central control unit?


Answer:

	Contact your regional Shure Distribution Center or Sales Representative to purchase a Feature License .xml file.You will need to have the serial number of the DIS-CCU on which the license will be installed because the license is generated for that specific DIS-CCU.
