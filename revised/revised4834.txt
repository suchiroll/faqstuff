Title: Feature Licenses for DIS-CCU
Id: 4834
Legacy Id: 6020

Question:
What Feature Licenses are available for the DIS-CCU central control unit?


Answer:

	Feature Licenses for the DIS-CCU facilitate system expansion.As of 2016, five Feature Licenses are available.

	
		FL6000 enables DCS 6000 Conference System mode for use with DCS 6000 conference units and components.
	
		FL6000-3800 enables expansion up to 3,800 participants.
	
		FL6000-INT-8 enables expansion to 8 interpretation languages instead of 4.
	
		FL6000-INT-16 enables expansion to 16 interpretation languages instead of 4.
	
		FL6000-INT-31 enables expansion to 31 interpretation languages instead of 4.
