Title: DC 6120 P replacement
Id: 4841
Legacy Id: 6027

Question:
What is the replacement for the discontinued DC 6120 P conference unit?


Answer:

	The DC 6190 P conference unit is the same size and has the same features as the DC 6120 P.Plus, the DC 6190 P has a headphone jack, volume control, and interpretation language channel selector, and can be configured to operate in dual delegate mode to be shared by two participants.
