Title: Replacement for PS CU power supply
Id: 4844
Legacy Id: 6030

Question:
What is the replacement for the discontinued PS CU power supply for the CU 5905, CU 6105, and CU 6110 central units?


Answer:

	The PS CU power supply is replaced by one of 11 region-specific power supplies, each of which includes a cord with the appropriate connector for that country/region.These models are:

	
		PS-CCU-AR (Argentina)
	
		PS-CCU-AZ (Australia)
	
		PS-CCU-BR (Brazil)
	
		PS-CCU-CHN (China)
	
		PS-CCU-E (Europe)
	
		PS-CCU-IN (India)
	
		PS-CCU-J (Japan)
	
		PS-CCU-K (Korea)
	
		PS-CCU-TW (Taiwan)
	
		PS-CCU-UK (United Kingdom)
	
		PS-CCU-US (USA)
