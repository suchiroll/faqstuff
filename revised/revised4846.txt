Title: Does DIS-CCU require Feature License for VOX?
Id: 4846
Legacy Id: 6032

Question:
Is a Feature license required to enable the VOX voice-activation feature on the DIS-CCU central control unit?


Answer:

	No.The VOX feature is now standard on all DIS-CCUs, when used with either 5900-series or 6000-series discussion/conference units.
