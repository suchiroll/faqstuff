Title: Firmware v8.0.1 and legacy DIS central units
Id: 4849
Legacy Id: 6035

Question:
Which central units is Firmware version 8.0.1 compatible with?


Answer:

	Firmware version 8.0.1 is compatible with the DIS-CCU, CU 5905, CU 6105, and CU 6110.It is not compatible with earlier central units.
