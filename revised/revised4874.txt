Title: Gold Layer on a Condenser Diaphragm
Id: 4874
Legacy Id: 6063

Question:
How thick is the gold layer on a condenser microphone diaphragm?


Answer:

	A typical layer thickness is around 400 Angstroms of gold on a diaphragm. This equals a thickness of 0.00000156 inch.

	The Angstrom unitis one ten-billionth of a meter; or 0.0000000039 inch.
