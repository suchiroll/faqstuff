Title: Motiv MV88 as Monaural Microphone
Id: 4875
Legacy Id: 6064

Question:
How can I set the MV88 to record in mono using only the Mid mic?


Answer:

	1) Go to 'Motiv Settings' in the MV88 app
	
	2) Swipe right on the polar pattern options to choose: stereo (the default); mono cardioid; mono bidirectional, or raw mid-side.
