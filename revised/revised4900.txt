Title: Shure Microphone Model G2
Id: 4900
Legacy Id: 6158

Question:
Please provide information on this model.


Answer:

	The model G2 was an OEM variation of the model 701D. It is not known what manufacturer purchased the model G2 from Shure.
	
	The crystal microphone 701D can be viewed in the 1939 Shure catalog.
