Title: Microphones for singing with a heavy metal band?
Id: 491
Legacy Id: 501

Question:
Which microphones in both wire and wireless, would be good for singing with a heavy metal sound requiring screaming?

Answer:

	We suggest the Beta 58A as the first choice, and the SM58 as the second choice. Both of these wired microphone are available in wireless systems. Your local Shure dealer will help you decide which will work better for your requirements.
