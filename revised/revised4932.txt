Question:
Can I connect WB98H/C (with TA4F) to a Line6 V55-BP transmitter?



Answer:

	Based on a Line 6 document supplied to Shure, theWB98H/C should work properly with the Line 6 transmitter. The connectors and the wiring schemes appear compatible.
