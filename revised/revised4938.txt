Title: Motiv Model Number Changes - June 2016
Id: 4938
Legacy Id: 6199

Question:
What are the new Motiv model numbers?


Answer:

	The Motiv external packaging has been updated.  The Motiv products have not changed. 
	
	As of June 1, 2016:
	MV5-B-LTG is now MV5/A-B-LTG.
	
	MV5-LTG is now MV5/A-LTG.
	
	MV51 is now MV51/A.
	
	MV88 is now MV88/A.
	
	MVi-LTG is now MVi/A-LTG.
	
	MVL is now MVL/A.
