Title: Shure OEM Microphones made for Montgomery Ward
Id: 4955
Legacy Id: 6220

Question:
Did Shure make microphones that were sold by Montgomery Ward?


Answer:

	Yes. Airline was the brand of electronic products sold by Montgomery Ward. Shure made three OEM Airline branded models: 62-7714, 62-7715, 62-7716.The microphones were sold as complementary accessories to Airline amplifiers and electric guitars.
