Title: MXA910 and MXA310 automix priority behavior
Id: 4998
Legacy Id: 6266

Question:
How does "automixing" priority work on the MXA310 and MXA910?


Answer:

	Priority works just like the Chairperson Override feature in the SCM820, where a channel with priority will activate even if the NOM (number of open mics) has been filled.
