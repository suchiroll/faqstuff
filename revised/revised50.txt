Title: Can a V15VxMR stylus replace a V15IV-MR stylus?
Id: 50
Legacy Id: 50

Question:
Can a V15VxMR stylus (model VN5xMR) be used to replace a V15IV-MR stylus?


Answer:

	Unfortunately no. The V15VxMR has a different sized stylus shaft than the V15IV-MR and wont fit in the cartridge.
