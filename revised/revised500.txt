Title: SM83A vs. MX183
Id: 500
Legacy Id: 510

Question:
Which of these mics is better?

Answer:

	The SM83A and the MX183 are different generations of omnidirectional lavalier mics. The SM83A is discontinued; MX183 is the current version. We think the MX183 sounds better than the SM83A.
	
	The WL83A and the WL183 are the wireless versions of these microphone.
