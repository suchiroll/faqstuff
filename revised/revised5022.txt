Title: SystemOn Language support
Id: 5022
Legacy Id: 6314

Question:
Is SystemOn available in different languages?


Answer:

	Yes. SystemOn software is available in multiple languages. Individual users can also change their language preference within their user profile / account as needed.
	
	Entitlement emails sent via the Software Portal software.shure.com, and the Software Portal itself are available in English only. Additional languages may be released later, depending on user demands.
