Title: Maximum channel count for ULX-D and QLX-D in the VHF band.
Id: 5033
Legacy Id: 6325

Question:
What is the maximum number of ULX-D or QLX-D channels I can use in the V50 VHF band?


Answer:

	Normal mode: 17 channels per 6 MHz TV channel
	HD mode (ULX-D only): 47 channels per 6 MHz TV channel
