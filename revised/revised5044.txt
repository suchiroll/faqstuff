Title: Model CX50 OEM Microphone
Id: 5044
Legacy Id: 6337

Question:
Which Shure model was the basis of the CX50 OEM microphone?


Answer:

	The CX50 was an OEM variation of the standard Shure crystral microphone Model 708A Stratoliner. The 708A may be viewed in the Shure 1940 catalog: Vintage Shure Catalogs
