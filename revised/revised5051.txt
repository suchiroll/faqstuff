Title: WA653 Mic Flag Extender Kit
Id: 5051
Legacy Id: 6348

Question:
What is the WA653 accessory?


Answer:

	WA653 Mic Flag Extender Kit
	
	Introduced in January 2017, the WA653 Microphone Flag Kit extends a Shure wireless transmitter handle to accommodate the length of a microphone station ID flag.The kit consists of a 1 inch microphone flag extender and a 1.5 inch microphone flag extender. The most common applications include Axient and UHF-R wireless systems. Microphone station ID flags are not included in the WA653 kit.
