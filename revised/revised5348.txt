Title: SE115 Discontinuation
Id: 5348
Legacy Id: 4098

Question:
SE115 Discontinuation

Answer:

	The SE115 earphones were discontinued March 1, 2011. The final U.S retail price (all models) was $120:
	SE115-K (black)
	SE115-BLU (blue)
	SE115-RD (red)
	SE115-PNK (pink)
	SE115-CL (clear)
