Title: Current Draw - UA830, UA870, UA874, UABIAST
Id: 5371
Legacy Id: 5392

Question:
Current Draw - UA830, UA870, UA874, UABIAST

Answer:

	UA830 inline antenna amplifier: maximum current draw of 125mA @12Vdc
	UA870 directional antenna with RF amplifier: maximum current draw of 79mA @12Vdc
	UA874 directional antenna with RF amplifier: maximum current draw of 60mA @12Vdc
	PS23 power supply is rated at 12Vdc @ 400ma.  This is used with the UABIAST.
