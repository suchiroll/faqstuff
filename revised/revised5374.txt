Title: CVO Microphone Dimensions
Id: 5374
Legacy Id: 5397

Question:
CVO Microphone Dimensions

Answer:

	Mic element diameter: 15 mm (0.6 inch)
	Mic element length: 55 mm (2.9 inches)
	Preamp diameter: 24 mm (0.9 inch)
	Preamp length: 123 mm (4.8 inches)
	Cable length from mic element to preamp: 7.6 m (25 feet)
