Title: WA335 and WA336
Id: 5376
Legacy Id: 5409

Question:
WA335 and WA336

Answer:

	The WA335 is for the UR1M transmitter.The WA335 is employed to replace the the transmitters TA4M connector with a 3-pin LEMO connector.This modification requires unsoldering/soldering of the printed circuit board.
	
	The WA336 is the LEMO connector for the cable of the lapel mic that must mate to the modified UR1M transmitter.
