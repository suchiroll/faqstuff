Title: VP83 and VP83F Audio Output Configuration
Id: 5379
Legacy Id: 5419

Question:
VP83 and VP83F Audio Output Configuration

Answer:

	VP83 and VP83F Audio Output to Camera: Tip and Ring are the same monaural signal (dual mono); Sleeve is ground.
	
	VP83F Headphone Output: Tip = left; Ring = right; Sleeve =ground. The Left and Right share the same signal, but are driven from separate output stages. This provides a louder signal in the headphones.
