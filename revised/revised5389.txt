Title: WBH54 incompatibility with Shure BLX wireless systems
Id: 5389
Legacy Id: 5565

Question:
WBH54 incompatibility with Shure BLX wireless systems

Answer:

	Please note that the WBH54 head-worn microphones will not operate properly with BLX transmitters. The symptoms are poor audio quality and/or a high level of noise.
