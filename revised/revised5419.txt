Title: Shure OEM Microphone for Kustom Products / Ross Incorporated
Id: 5419
Legacy Id: 6402

Question:
Did Shure ever make an OEM microphone for Kustom PA Systems, made by Ross Incorporated in Chanute, Kansas?

Answer:

	Yes. A Kustom catalog from the mid-1960s shows a Shure OEM, or Private Label, microphone. The model was named the Kustom Microphone and carried a U.S. retail price of $59.00. It was based on the Shure standard model 585.
