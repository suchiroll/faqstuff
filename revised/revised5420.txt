Title: Shure OEM Microphones for Heathkit
Id: 5420
Legacy Id: 6401

Question:
Did Shure ever make microphones for Heathkit?

Answer:

	Yes.A Heathkit catalog from October 1966 shows three Shure OEM, or Private Label, microphones.
	
	Model GDP-66 at $36.46 U.S. Retail - based on the standard model 533
	Model GDP-76 at $45.28 U.S. Retail - based on the standard model 585
	Model GDP-66 at $62.92 U.S. Retail - based on the standard model 565
