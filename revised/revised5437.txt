Title: Shure Alta Pro 4
Id: 5437
Legacy Id: 6382

Question:
Do you have information on this cartridge?

Answer:

	This model was private label, made by Shure and packaged for a specific hi-fi retailer.

	The Alta Pro 4 uses the N92E stylus. Thus, the body was the M92E. Therefore, the specs are the same as the M92E.
