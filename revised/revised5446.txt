Title: RMCE-BT1 compatible with my Bluetooth device?
Id: 5446
Legacy Id: 

Question:
Is the RMCE-BT1 compatible with my Bluetooth device?

Answer:
The RMCE-BT1 uses the Bluetooth 4.1 standard, which offers reliable, short-range wireless connectivity and performs well when used near LTE and 4G devices.  Bluetooth 4.1 is backwards-compatible with all Bluetooth source devices.
