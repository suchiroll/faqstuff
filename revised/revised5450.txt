Title: RMCE-BT1 work with all Shure earphone models?
Id: 5450
Legacy Id: 

Question:
Does the RMCE-BT1 work with all Shure earphone models?

Answer:
The RMCE-BT1 features MMCX connectors, making it compatible with all Shure detachable SE earphones models except for the SE112.  The BT1 adapter is included with SE215-BT1 and SE112-BT1 models.
