Title: ULX-D, QLX-D, AXT400 control with Yamaha QL and CL consoles
Id: 5471
Legacy Id: 

Question:
How do I setup receivers and Yamaha console for control?

Answer:
Yamaha CL and QL consoles can control and show the parameters for the ULXD4D, ULXD4Q, ULXD4, QLXD4, and AXT400.
Yamaha has written up documentation on setting this up. Please refer to their documentation at:
http://www.yamahaproaudio.com/global/en/products/mixers/ql/selftraining.jsp
