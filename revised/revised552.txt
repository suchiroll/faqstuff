Title: Beta 87A requires two monitors to have balanced  sound in mono application?
Id: 552
Legacy Id: 563

Question:
Please explain why the Beta 87A can not have a monitor placed directly in front of the user.


Answer:

	Because the Beta 87A has a supercardioid polar pattern. For the best explaination, download the following booklet:
	
	Microphone Techniques for Music - Live Sound Reinforcement

	
	Page 10 of the book has a section called Using Polar Patterns to Reject Unwanted Sounds. This section has a great explanation and diagrams to answer your question.
