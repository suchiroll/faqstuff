Question:
Do you still make the R25XT and was it a good better or best needle. and finally if not what is a good replacement for it?

Answer:

	The Shure N75EJ-T2 stylus is available from Shure Parts. It will fit the R25XT body. Call Shure Parts at 800-516-2525 to purchase the N75EJ-T2.
