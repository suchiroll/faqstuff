Title: Compatibility between mic output and guitar amp input
Id: 636
Legacy Id: 653

Question:
Will a low impedance mic work with a guitar amp through the regular input jack?

Answer:

	The impedance will not be an issue, but if the sensitivity of the input jack is not similar to the microphone, then there will be a large level difference.
