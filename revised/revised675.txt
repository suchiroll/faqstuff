Title: Will the U4 fit in the WA590 case?
Id: 675
Legacy Id: 692

Question:
Will this case hold BOTH the Body pack transmitter (with WL184) and the SM58 hand transmitter with the receiver all in one?

Answer:

	No. The U4 receiver will not fit in the WA590 carrying case. The WA590 can be used with any of our 1/2 rack size receivers.
