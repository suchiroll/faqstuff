Title: FP33 Fuse
Id: 700
Legacy Id: 717

Question:
What is the fuse for the FP33? Where can it be purchased?

Answer:

	An FP33 replacement fuse is available from Shure Parts at 800-516-2525. The Shure part number is 187AJ06A.
	
	The fuse is made by Littelfuse, part number R451.002. This surface mount fuse is rated at 2A at 125V. The fuse is also available from Mouser Electronics at 800-346-6873. The Mouser part number is 5762-451002.
