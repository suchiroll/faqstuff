Title: When to replace the stylus?
Id: 709
Legacy Id: 727

Question:
How can you tell if you need to replace the stylus on a cartridge?

Answer:

	One of four ways:
	1. Replace the stylus if the stylus shaft is bent or crooked.
	2. Replace the stylus if the total playing time of the stylus is over 500 hours.
	3. Replace the stylus if the audio quality is poor not matter what the recording.
	4. Replace the stylus if the stylus shows serious wear when examined under a stylus inspection microscope.
