Title: Theater Audio - Securing the MX150 into the hairline
Id: 754
Legacy Id: 774

Question:
What is the best way of securing a MX150 mic into the hairline for stagework and theater use?


Answer:

	Bobby pins, hair pins, and hair clips can all be used to secure lavalier microphones into the hair. These can all be found at at beauty supply store or local department store. If you are using a wig, the cable can be sewn to the wig material.
