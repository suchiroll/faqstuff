Title: Theater Audio - Belt pouches for wireless transmitters
Id: 755
Legacy Id: 775

Question:
Where can I find pouches to hold wireless transmitters?


Answer:

	Shure offers the WA570A body-pack belt pouch and WA620 arm pouch.They aremade of Neoprene and fit all Shure body-pack transmitters.
	
	Pouches made specifically for theater applications are available from Masque Sound. The product name is Pack Rat and is available in black or beige.
