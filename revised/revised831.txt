Title: V15 Type IV tracking force
Id: 831
Legacy Id: 854

Question:
I have the Shure V 15 Type IV cartridge with the original stylus. Please confirm the proper tracking force.


Answer:

	Tracking force: minimum - 1.25 grams maximum - 1.75 (With Dynamic Stabilizer operating)
	
	The V15 Type IV was discontinued in 1982 with the introduction of the V15 Type V.
