Title: Condenser microphones with switchable polar patterns
Id: 877
Legacy Id: 904

Question:
Which condenser microphones have switchable polar patterns? I only want to buy 2 mics but want to achieve xy pair mid+side and spaced omni's. Is this possible?








Answer:

	Yes, using the KSM44. It is switchable between omni, cardioid, and bidirectional.
