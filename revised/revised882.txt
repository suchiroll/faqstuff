Title: Wildtrack - mics for sound effects
Id: 882
Legacy Id: 909

Question:
Could you please advise me of most reasonable priced Shure microphone suitable for wildtrack effect recording in the field using a portable recorder? The sound sources will be birds, trains, cars, church bells etc..


Answer:

	Check out the Shure VP88 and the Shure SM63.
	
	However, your ears must make the final decision. We can only make recommendations on models for you to audition.
	
	For a complete listing of microphones that we recommend for ambient recording, see our online Microphone Techniques for Recording.
