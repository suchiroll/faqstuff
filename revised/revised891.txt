Title: Model 300 ribbon mic self-noise
Id: 891
Legacy Id: 918

Question:
Can you tell me the "self-noise" of the model 300 microphone? (I know you no longer make it.). Thanks.

Answer:

	At 70 degrees F and with an actual internal impedance of 260 ohms, the self-noise of the model 300 is -130.7 dBV.
