Title: Suggestions for percussion mics
Id: 893
Legacy Id: 921

Question:
What would you recommend for micing this setup? 1 set of congas,1 set of bongos, & assorted shakers,cymbals,(overhead mic),etc. Low-intermediate budget for playing live.

Answer:

	SM57s for the congas and bongos. PG81 for the shakers and cymbals.
