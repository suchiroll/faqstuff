Title: Wiring of VP88 5-pin XLR jack
Id: 948
Legacy Id: 976

Question:
The VP88 stereo microphone uses a 5-pin XLR Jack. How is it wired?


Answer:

	The VP88 provides two balanced outputs as follows:
	
	Pin 1: Shield/ground
	Pin 2: left channel +
	Pin 3: left channel -
	Pin 4: right channel +
	Pin 5: right channel -
