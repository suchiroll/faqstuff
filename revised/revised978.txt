Title: Parts catalog for wireless?
Id: 978
Legacy Id: 1007

Question:
How do I find a parts catalog for wireless product?


Answer:

	These items can be found in print in the Shure product catalog.
	
	There is no catalog for piece part items like battery covers, on/off switches, etc. Items such as these can be ordered from the Shure Service Department. For information on purchasing these items, please contact the Service Department directly.
	
	Toll-free at 800-516-2525 or by email at serviceweb@shure.com
	The hours are from Monday through Friday, 7:30am to 4pm Central Time.
