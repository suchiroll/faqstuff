Title: Cartridge for Realistic turntable Lab 2200
Id: 1002
Legacy Id: 1031

Question:
I have a Realistic turn table, model Lab 2200 and need a replacement stylus for the cartridge. The cartridge has a Realistic label with p/n 623. The cartridge mounting is P-type, that is four pins out the back with a screw hole through the pin block to secure to the tone arm. The stylus clips on to the cartridge.


Answer:

	Shure has a P-mount cartridge available: model M92E.
