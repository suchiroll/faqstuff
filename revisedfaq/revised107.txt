Question:
I play clarinet and tenor sax. I'd like to know which is your best wireless mic for both instruments.


Answer:

	The microphone you would be looking for is the Beta 98H or PGA98H. The Beta 98H or PGA98H can be ordered/purchased with several of our wireless systems.
