Title: VHF Quarter Wave Antenna Lengths and  Tip Colors
Id: 1106
Legacy Id: 1142

Question:
What does the antenna tip color mean? And how long is each?


Answer:

	Red tips (15 3/4 inches)designate frequencies between 169 MHz to 185 MHz.

	
	Green tips (14 5/8 inches) designate frequencies between 185 MHz to 200 MHz.

	
	Blue tips (13 1/4 inches)designate frequencies between 200 MHz to 215 MHz
