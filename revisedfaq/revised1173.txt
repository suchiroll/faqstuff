Title: M267 Mix Bus
Id: 1173
Legacy Id: 1215

Question:
Can you use the MIX BUS with other mixers which DON'T have one? Is it a line-level signal? is it an input or output or both depending upon the application?


Answer:

	The Mix Bus can be used as an additional unbuffered output.
	
	The mix bus is pre-Master gain control and line level.
