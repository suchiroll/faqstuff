Title: Using the headphone jack of a mixer for connecting the P4HW
Id: 1191
Legacy Id: 1235

Question:
If using the headphone jack of a mixer for my source to my P4HW, how would the cable need to be wired? Going from a TRS 1/4" male to the left  right XLR of the belt pack.


Answer:

	1/4 Tip -- Left XLR Pin 2
	1/4 Ring -- Right XLR Pin 2
	1/4 Sleeve -- Left XLR Pin 1  3and Right XLR Pin 1  3
	
	Adapter cables can be purchased from Markertek.
