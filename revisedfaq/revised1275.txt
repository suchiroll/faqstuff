Title: SCM810 removable block connectors
Id: 1275
Legacy Id: 1323

Question:
What is the model no. for your removable block connectors for the SCM810 mixer, what is the price, and do you have them in stock to purchase?


Answer:

	The Shure part number for a single block connector is 95A8580. Pricing and availability can be obtained by calling Shure Parts at 800-516-2525.
	
	The commercial equivalent for the connector is Augat 796634-03.

	

	The pin spacing on the connector is 5.08 mm.
