Title: Replacement for 2215 stylus
Id: 1332
Legacy Id: 1384

Question:
Looking for a replacement stylus. The number on my stylus is SHURE 2225 and the number on my cartridge is SHURE 2215 and it is a Standard Mount. Thank you for your help.


Answer:

	The replacement for the 2215 stylus is the N75B Type 2.
