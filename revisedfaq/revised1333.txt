Title: Vocal Master VA300-S and VA301-S Loudspeakers
Id: 1333
Legacy Id: 1385

Question:
I need to replaced loudspeakers in my Vocal Master column speaker. What do I need?



Answer:

	The 10 full range loudspeaker is 16 ohms and must handle 25 watts (minimum) or greater.

	The 8 full range loudspeaker is 8 ohms and must handle 25 watts (minimum) or greater.
	
	Possible source for replacement loudspeakers: www.parts-express.com
