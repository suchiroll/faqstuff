Title: Replacement for cartridge V15ME on SME 3012 tone arm
Id: 1335
Legacy Id: 1387

Question:
Please advise me on a replacement cartridge for Shure V15ME mounted on SME 3012 arm and Thorens 124/II turntable?


Answer:

	Assuming that your tone arm can track at .75 to 1.5 grams, we suggest the M97xE.
