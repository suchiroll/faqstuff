Title: Difference between WL93 and WL184
Id: 1345
Legacy Id: 1397

Question:
Can you please tell me the difference between the WL184 lavalier microphone and the WL93 lavalier? I see that the only thing different between the UT14/93 system and the UT14/84 system is the microphone. Thanks.


Answer:

	The WL93 is omnidirectional; the WL184 is supercardioid.
