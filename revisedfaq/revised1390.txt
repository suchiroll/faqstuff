Title: What is the subsonic filter on the KSM44?
Id: 1390
Legacy Id: 1442

Question:
What kind of filter is the KSM44 subsonic filter that eliminates rumble from mechanical vibration below 17 Hz?


Answer:

	The KSM44 sub-sonic filter is an active electronic three pole filter, with a slope of 18 dB per octave, and with 17 Hz being the 3dB down point.
