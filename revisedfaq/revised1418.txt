Title: FP24 use
Id: 1418
Legacy Id: 1471

Question:
I am mech engineer using a DAT tape recorder with a stereo microphone with two 3 pin XLR microphone connectors. Can I use the FP24 as pre amp with a phantom voltage and also use the FP24 as an input for a power amplifier?



Answer:

	Yes, the FP24 will provide both functions you require.
