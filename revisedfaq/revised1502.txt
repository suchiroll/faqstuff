Legacy Id: 1555

Question:
I have a Shure AXS5 Mic I purchased from Guitar Center. When I pull out the microphone cable at the base of the mic, the whole pin assembly comes with it if I'm not careful. How can I fix it myself?


Answer:

	PG81, SM94, 849, BG 5.1, or BG 4.1 XLR connector is loose
