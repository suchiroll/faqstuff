Title: M44-7 setup on Techniques 1200 M3D
Id: 167
Legacy Id: 168

Question:
The new 1200 s have an extra 4 gram headshell weight and a different tonearm weight. What would the setup be with the 44-7 s on the new 1200 m3d s?

Answer:

	The Shure SFG-2 Tracking Force Gauge, available through your Shure dealer, provides a simple way to accurately set tracking force.
