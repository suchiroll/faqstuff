Title: Phantom Power for 16AM mic
Id: 1722
Legacy Id: 1781

Question:
I took out the Size AA Battery from the 16AM and tried to operate it using Phantom Power (48V) from my mixer. I get no sound. Is the 16AM able to operate with Phantom Power?


Answer:

	No, it will not operate on phantom. The 16AM only operates from the AA battery.
