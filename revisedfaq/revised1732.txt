Title: N75 stylus with Shure V15 Type II
Id: 1732
Legacy Id: 1791

Question:
Can you confirm that M75 compatible styli could be used in the V15 Type II?


Answer:

	Yes, the N75 replacement styli line will fit the V15 Type II body. The two N75 models available are:

	
	N75ED Type 2 - elliptical stylus; 0.75 - 1.5 grams tracking.
	N75B Type 2 - spherical stylus; 1.5 to 3 grams tracking.
