Title: Cable for SM98A to a wireless transmitter
Id: 1735
Legacy Id: 1794

Question:
The SM98A has 3-pin connector, and transmitters like the UT1 have a 4-pin. Are there any cables to connect them? adapter?

Answer:

	To connect the SM98A mic directly to a Shure wireless transmitter, use the Shure cable 90A4039. In this set-up, do not use the SM98 in-line preamp.
