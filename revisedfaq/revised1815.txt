Title: What is "voice over"?
Id: 1815
Legacy Id: 1875

Question:
Recently I saw KSM27 new product from your web site and thought it is very attractive.


I have some question about it, I found its applications when I read it on your web.


What is voice-over? Please explain it. I am a student who is very enthusiastic for Shure microphones.


Answer:

	A DJ at a radio station is doing voice over. The announcer on a commercial is doing voice over. The actor doing character voices for a cartoon, is doing voice over.
	
	In general, someone talking into a microphone in a controlled studio is considered voice over.
