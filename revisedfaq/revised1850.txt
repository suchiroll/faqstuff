Title: Can I use two E1's with one PSM600 receiver?
Id: 1850
Legacy Id: 1910

Question:
I would like to know if I could split the output signal to use two E1's with one PSM600 receiver for a duo acoustic setup?

Answer:

	Yes, it will work. But because the audio signal is now divided between two E1 units, neither will be as loud as when using a single E1 unit.
