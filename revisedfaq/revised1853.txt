Question:
Hello, I recently acquired an Onkyo record player, it has Shure cartridge, model# R25XT, if I'm unable to find the recommended replacement needle (NDC EJ), is there another needle that will replace this one, or another option that you could recommend?

	Use the Shure model N75B Type 2.
