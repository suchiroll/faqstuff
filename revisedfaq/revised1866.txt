Question:
Which pair of WA380 antennas would I purchase to cover all the possible frequenices from 169.00 to 230.00.


WA380A

WA380B

WA380C


The pair of antennas could be mounted on a WA470 or WA450 and have up to 12 VHF recceivers with various frequenices.


Answer:

	To cover the entire frequency range 169.445-210.6, we recommend using one WA380A and one WA380C.
