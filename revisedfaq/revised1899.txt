Question:
I am a student studying 'Audio Engineering' with the SAE College in London. My main thesis is: What has made the Shure SM58 Microphone so popular in both studio and live environment?


Answer:

	1) Great sound
	2) Reasonable price
	3) Nearly indestructable
	
	All three qualities in one microphone make for a very popular product.
