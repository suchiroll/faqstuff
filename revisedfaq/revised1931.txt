Title: Radio Shack Shure Mic Model 33-3010A
Id: 1931
Legacy Id: 1993

Question:
A few years ago my girlfriend bought me a microphone. I just found it in a box and was wondering if you could give me any spec information.


Radio Shack Pro-3010

Shure Unidirectional Dynamic Microphone

33-3010 Mexico


Answer:

	The Radio Shack/Shure Microphone Model 33-3010A is:
	
	Unidirectional
	Designed for speech or vocals
	Dynamic element
	150 ohms impedance
	XLR male connector
	Balanced output
	Output level of -73.5 dBV at 74 dBSPL
	
	It was designed specifically for Radio Shack.
