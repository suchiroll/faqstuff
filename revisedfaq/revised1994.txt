Question:
Please could you send me the wiring diagram of the WA302 Tiny QG to 1/4" connector.


Answer:

	

	
		
			
				1/4 Phone Plug
			
				Tiny QG Connector (TA4F)
		
		
			
				Tip (Red and Black)
			
				Pin 3
		
		
			
				Sleeve (Shield)
			
				Pin 1
		
		
			
				
			
				Pins 2 and 4: not connected
