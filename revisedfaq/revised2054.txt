Title: Shure PRO-10 Phono Cartridge replacement
Id: 2054
Legacy Id: 2119

Question:
Is the PRO-10 still available as replacement, and if so, how much? Does it include the stylus? If not, is the M92E the best choice?


It is installed as a P mount in a Sony record player.


Answer:

	The PRO-10 cartridge is not available. The N111E is the replacement stylus.
	
	If you desire a new cartridge, the M92E is a good choice.
