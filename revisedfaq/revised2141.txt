Title: How do I mike cymbals?
Id: 2141
Legacy Id: 2212

Question:
I am ordering your drum mic package, and I was wondering if they would be good enough to mic cymbals for recording?


Answer:

	Cymbals are best miked by using condensor mics. The drum package does not contain a condensor mics. To see which microphones we recommend for cymbals, please see our online Selection Guides.
