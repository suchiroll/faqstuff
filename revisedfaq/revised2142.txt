Title: Is the N78S stylus compatible with an M95ED cartridge?
Id: 2142
Legacy Id: 2213

Question:
I have an M95ED cartridge (actually the lettering is ME95ED where the leftmost "E" is tiny and flush with the top of the "M"). Is it compatible with the N78S stylus?


Answer:

	Yes, the N78S will fit the M95 body. Be careful when installing the N78S as it is a tight fit.
