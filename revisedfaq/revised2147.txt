Title: Number of inputs on P7T transmitter
Id: 2147
Legacy Id: 2218

Question:
I want to clarify how many audio inputs are on the 700 series, two for stereo or mixmode?


Answer:

	The PSM 700 tranmitter has two inputs - left channel and right channel. The P7T transmitter can send one stereo signal, or two monaural signals. The P7R receiver can receive one stereo signal or two monoaural signals.
