Title: Which styli fit the V15VxMR body?
Id: 2177
Legacy Id: 2250

Question:
Which styli fit the V15VxMR body?


Answer:

	The VN5xMR - tracks at 1.0 gram; tip size is 0.15 x 3.0 mils
	
	N97xE - tracks at 0.75 to 1.5 grams; tip size is 0.2 x 0.7 mils
	
	N44-7X - tracks at 1.5 to 3.0 grams; tip radius is 0.7 mils
	
	SS35C - tracks at 4.0 to 5.0 grams; tip radius is 0.7 mils
	
	N78S - tracks at 1.5 to 3.0 grams; tip radius is 2.5 mils
