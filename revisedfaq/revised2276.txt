Title: Where can I find a cable for a model 540 microphone?
Id: 2276
Legacy Id: 2357

Question:
I came into possession of a 540 series II mic and would like a cable to use with it.


Answer:

	The model 540 Series II needs an Amphenol MC3M on the cable. We do not carry these connectors.
	
	You can also get pre-wired cables from Tom Ellis - U.S. phone: 214-328-3225.
