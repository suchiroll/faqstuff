Title: DPA Mic to a Shure wireless transmitter?
Id: 2284
Legacy Id: 2366

Question:
How do I connect a DPA Mic to a Shure wireless transmitter?


Answer:

	Most of the DPA miniature mics use a Micro-Dot connector. To connect to a Shure transmitter with a TA4M input, use the DPA adapter DAD6010. This adapter has a Micro-Dot input and a TA4F output.
