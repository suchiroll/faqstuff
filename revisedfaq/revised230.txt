Title: L11 Transmitters working with LX receivers?
Id: 230
Legacy Id: 235

Question:
My client recently had two receivers stolen during a break in.  Nobody knows what model receivers they were.  The thieves did not take the two transmitters.  Transmitters are model L11.  Freqs are: 183.6 and 182.2  Are LX series receivers compatible with these transmitters/freqs?

Answer:

	Yes, an LX receiver will work properly with an L11 transmitter.
