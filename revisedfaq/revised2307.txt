Question:
I have a M44E cartridge. I am trying to find tech specs on it.


Answer:

	M44E
	Response: 20 to 20,000 Hz
	Output voltage: 9.5 mV per channel
	Separation: 20dB at 1,000 Hz
	Tracking force: 1.75 to 4 grams
	Stylus: biradial (.0004 x .0007 inch)
	Weight: 6.5 grams
	Optimal load per channel: 47,000 ohms in parallel with 400 to 500 picofarads
