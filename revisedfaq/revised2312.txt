Title: MX391 mic for Intel TeamStation
Id: 2312
Legacy Id: 2398

Question:
How is the Shure MX391 microphone wired for the Intel Team Station?


Answer:

	MX391 Variation for Intel TeamStation
	
	1. Does not include in-line preamp
	
	2. Switchcraft TA4F connector replaced by Switchcraft TA3F. The TA3F may be purchased at www.mouser.com
	
	3. TA3F wired as follows: Pin 1 - shield; pin 2 - bias (red wire); pin 3 - audio (black wire)
	
	4. Input on Intel TeamStation provides +5V bias to operate MX391 FET.
