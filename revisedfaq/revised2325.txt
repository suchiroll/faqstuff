Title: Shure SS78E elliptical stylus for SC35C?
Id: 2325
Legacy Id: 2411

Question:
We are looking for the following part: Shure SS78E elliptical stylus.


Answer:

	The SS78E is discontinued. It was designed to fit the SC35C cartridge and play 78 RPM records.
	The N78S stylus is available and is a direct replacement for the SS78E.
