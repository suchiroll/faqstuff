Title: SM98 cable to connect to a Shure Transmitter
Id: 2337
Legacy Id: 2423

Question:
What is the part number for the cable that connects the Shure SM98 mic capsule to a Shure transmitter with a four-pin TQG input?


Answer:

	The part number is 90A4039. The cable is 44 inches long. On one end is a 3 pin female TQG connector; on the other end is a 4 pin female TQG connector.
