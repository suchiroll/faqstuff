Question:
I have older model turntables, I'm looking for needle cartridge replacements turntable model is Gemini D.J.Q. 1300 Shure needle M950P


Answer:

	The replacement stylus for the M950P is the N96LT.
	
	To replace the entire M950P cartridge, purchase the model M92E.
