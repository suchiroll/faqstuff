Title: Shure E1 compatible with Nady E03 System
Id: 2355
Legacy Id: 2443

Question:
I purchased a set of Sure E1 earpieces and would like to know if there are any specific details needed to use them with a Nady E03 system.


Answer:

	The Nady must be able to drive a load of 16 ohms; that is the impedance of the E1 earphones. Ask this question of Nady.
