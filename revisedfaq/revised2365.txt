Title: Specified static compliance of M97xE and M44-7 cartridges
Id: 2365
Legacy Id: 2456

Question:
What is the compliance of the M97xE and the M44-7 cartridges?

Answer:

	The static compliance for the M97xE:
	0.000025 centimeter per dyne of force
	
	The static compliance for the M44-7:
	0.000020 centimeter per dyne of force
