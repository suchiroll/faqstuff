Title: Radio Shack R27E Cartridge
Id: 2383
Legacy Id: 2474

Question:
I have a defective R27E cartridge. What of your present products will replace the R27E? Also please include information on the stylus required for the replacement cartridge. Thank You.


Answer:

	The R27E was made by Shure for Radio Shack.
	
	The replacement stylus for the R27E is the model N75ED Type 2.
	
	To replace the entire cartridge, purchase the Shure model M92E.
