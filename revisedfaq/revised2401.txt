Title: PE54D -- Unidyne III
Id: 2401
Legacy Id: 2493

Question:
1. Does a new SM57 differ from this model specifically and the older Unidyne III models in general, or are they essentially the same microphone?

2. How does my old Unidyne III differ from my new 55SH Series II?


Answer:

	1. Does a new SM57 differ from this model specifically and the older Unidyne III models in general, or are they essentially the same microphone?
	* Essentially, the same.
	
	2. How does my old Unidyne III differ from my new 55SH Series II?
	* The Unidyne III is a better mic than the 55SH Series II: better frequency response, better shock isolation, better polar pattern.
