Title: M91ED and M93E - stylus compatibility?
Id: 2410
Legacy Id: 2502

Question:
Is stylus of M91ED and M93E exchangeable? The shape looks same? What is the track force for M93E's (N93E) stylus?


Answer:

	Is stylus of M91ED and M93E exchangeable?
	* Yes.
	
	What is the track force for M93Es (N93E) stylus?
	* 1.5 grams to 3 grams.
