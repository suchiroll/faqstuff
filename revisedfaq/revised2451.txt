Title: Changing frequency on UT system.
Id: 2451
Legacy Id: 2546

Question:
Is it possible to have the frequency changed on a UT-transmitter/reciever?

We have one with frequency at 624.300 MHz that receives an interference

frequency.



Answer:

	Yes, Shure Service can change the frequency of your system for a fee. Contact them at 800-516-2525 or 847-866-2699.
	
	Make certain to have the frequencies of any other wireless systems when you call.
