Title: LX, U, ULX current draw
Id: 2474
Legacy Id: 2569

Question:
How can I find out what the current draw of


(a) the handheld transmitters


and


(b) the beltback transmitters


of the following systems is: LX, ULX, UHF?



Answer:

	The handheld and bodypack transmitters of individual systems have the same current draw.
	
	LX: 35 mA maximum
	ULX: 65 mA maximum
	UHF: 120-130 mA
