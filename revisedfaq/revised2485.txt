Title: UP4 Antenna Connectors
Id: 2485
Legacy Id: 2580

Question:
What is the antenna connector used on the UP4? Where can I purchase this connector and adapters?


Answer:

	The UP4 antenna connector is known as an SMA connector. It is a female.
	
	A supplier for SMA connections and adapters is: http://pasternack.com
	
	A right angle female/male SMA adapter is available: Pasternack part: PE9642
	
	A right angle SMA to BNC adapter is available: Pasternack part: PE9074
