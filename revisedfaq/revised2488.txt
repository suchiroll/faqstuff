Title: SM81 suffix and Beta87 suffix
Id: 2488
Legacy Id: 2583

Question:
Every microphone has a suffix letters. What do the stand for? Example: SM81-LC and Beta 87A.


Answer:

	It depends upon the model. There is no "master key" to this question.
	
	SM81-LC: LC means Less Cable
	
	Beta 87A: the A version has a larger diameter handle than the non-A version.
