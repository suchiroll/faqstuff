Title: A15LA or another type of pad?
Id: 2502
Legacy Id: 2598

Question:
Can A15LA be used between VCR output (mono RCA connection) to Mic input on mixer(balanced XLR)?? Now using adaptor cable to connect but level too high.


Answer:

	Yes, but the actual amount of A15LA attenuation depends upon the output impedance of the VCR.
