Title: Stromberg Carlson OEM Microphones
Id: 2515
Legacy Id: 2611

Question:
Did Shure make OEM microphones for Stromberg Carlson?


Answer:

	Yes. There was an OEM version of the Unidyne II Model 55S. The MC-41 was likelymanufactured between1951 to1961. The predecessor of the MC-41 was the model MC-31; it was based on the Unidyne Model 55, with the larger housing. MC = Moving Coil
	
	There was also the model MD-57, based on the Shure model 535.
