Title: Difference between the PG58-XLR and  PG58-QTR
Id: 2564
Legacy Id: 2662

Question:
I wanted to know what was the difference between buying the PG58-XLR and PG58-QTR? What is the difference between them?


Answer:

	The connector at the end of the cable: QTR = 1/4" male phone plug; XLR = 3 pin male XLR.
