Title: KSM32 and KSM44 output levels
Id: 2570
Legacy Id: 2669

Question:
What is the output level on the KSM32 and the KSM44?


Answer:

	KSM32:
	- 36dBV per Pascal
	
	KSM44:
	- 31dBV per Pascal - cardioid
	- 37dBV per Pascal - omnidirectional
	- 36dBV per Pascal - bidirectional
	
	One Pascal equals 94 dB SPL.
