Title: ME95ED Cartridge Specifications
Id: 2577
Legacy Id: 2676

Question:
I have an ME95ED cartridge fitted to my turntable. I would like to know the output in mV, and whether the cartridge is a moving magnet cartridge?


Answer:

	1) 4.7mV per channel at 1,000 Hz at 5 cm/second peak recorded velocity
	
	2) moving magnet
	
	3) 0.75 to 1.5 gram tracking range
