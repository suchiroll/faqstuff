Title: Shure lavalier mic to AKG body pack PT81
Id: 2615
Legacy Id: 2717

Question:
How is a Shure condenser lavalier, like the WL50, connected to an AKG PT81 body pack?


Answer:

	Shure shield wire (ground) to AKG pin 1.
	Shure black wire (audio) to AKG pin 2.
	Shure red wire (bias) to AKG pin 3.
	
	Solder a 10,000 ohm resistor between AKG pin 1 to AKG pin 2.
