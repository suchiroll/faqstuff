Title: KSM137 compared to SM81
Id: 2676
Legacy Id: 2780

Question:
Please compare the KSM137 to the SM81.


Answer:

	The SM81 is:
	1) longer in length
	2) has flatter response below 100 Hz
	3) has a flatter response above 5 kHz
	
	The KSM 137 is:
	1) quieter
	2) has a higher SPL overload point
	3) has a higher output level
