Title: Mic Holder for String Instruments
Id: 2678

Question:
I need a mic holder to mount a Shure lav mic to a violin. Any suggestions?


Answer:

	Yes, DPA Microphones offers the MHS6005 mic holder (five holders in a single package) that works with violin, viola, cello, and double bass.www.DPAMicrophones.comThis holder will work with Shure miniature lavalier microphones like the: MX150; SM93; WL93; MC50B; WL50; MC51B; WL51.
