Title: Will the VN35MR stylus fit the M97 body?
Id: 2680
Legacy Id: 2784

Question:
I have an old M97 body, currently using a N97HE stylus. My question is if I could use a VN35MR stylus in the M97 body? Or a VN5MR stylus, perhaps?


Answer:

	Sorry, but neither the VN35MR nor the VN5MR stylus work properly with the M97 body.
