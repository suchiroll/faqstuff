Question:
My 83 year old mother has a realistic lab 400 with a "r1000ed" broken needle. I've tried to look up its replacement and I think it is N75ED-t2.Is this r1000ed an original shure cartridge?


Answer:

	The stylus was the model N75ED-T2. The R1000ED is a Shure cartridge that was made for Radio Shack.
