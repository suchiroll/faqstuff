Title: Purchasing a R5 cartridge?
Id: 273
Legacy Id: 280

Question:
How and where can I obtain a "R5" cartridge?

Answer:

	All versions of the R5 cartridge are not a current models; all are discontinued. However, Shure Parts might have stock of the R5D and R5F cartridges. Both are high impedance. Call 800-516-2525 for pricing and availability.
