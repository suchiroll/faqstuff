Title: M44-5 replacement stylus
Id: 274
Legacy Id: 281

Question:
I own a record player that uses the M44-5. I am in desperate need of a replacement stylus. Which of your current styli are compatible with the M44-5?


Answer:

	The replacement stylus for the M44-5 cartridge is the N44G. Order the N44G stylus from Shure Parts at 800-516-2525 or 847-600-8699.
