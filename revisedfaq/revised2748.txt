Title: 3 mil stylus for 78 RPM records
Id: 2748
Legacy Id: 2854

Question:
For which, if any, of the Shure stereo phono cartridges (current or old) can 3 mil (78 rpm) needles be obtained.


Answer:

	Shure does not offer a 3 mil stylus. The M78S cartridge, made to play 78 RPM records, has a 2.5 mil radius tip.
