Title: PGA58 Impedance - Low or High?
Id: 2763
Legacy Id: 2871

Question:
Are the PGA58's different in impedance when you get the 1/4" cable. Are the XLR and 1/4" both low impedance mics?


Answer:

	Both are low impedance. To obtain a high impedance PGA58, add the A85F transformer at the end of the XLR cable.
