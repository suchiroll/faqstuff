Title: Ultra 500 stylus
Id: 2787
Legacy Id: 2895

Question:
Will the replacement stylus of the Shure phono cartridge V15xMR fit the phono cartridge Shure ULTRA 500?


Answer:

	The correct replacement stylus for the Ultra 500 is the VN5MR.
	
	The VN5xMR does NOT work properly with the Ultra 500. The magnet in this stylus assembly does not align properly with the Ultra 500 pole pieces.
