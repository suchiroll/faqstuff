Title: Sennheiser ME 3 with Shure transmitter
Id: 2789
Legacy Id: 2897

Question:
How do I wire a Sennheiser ME 3 to a Shure transmitter?


Answer:

	This answer was provide by our colleagues at Sennheiser. Vielen Danke!
	
	Sennheiser ME 3 microphone to the TA4F connector that mates with a Shure transmitter:
	
	a) Yellow wire and shield to pin 1
	
	b) 8,200 ohm resistor between pin 2 and pin 3
	
	c) Jumper wire between pin 3 and pin 4
	
	d) Red wire to pin 4
