Title: VN2E Stylus
Id: 2796
Legacy Id: 2905

Question:
What is the specification of VN2E?


Answer:

	The VN2E can only be used with the V15 Type I.
	
	Specs: 0.75 to 1.5 tracking force; elliptical stylus - 0.0002 x 0.0009.

	Later versions of this stylus (1966and on)had the following dimensions: 0.0002 x 0.0007.
