Title: What is supplied in the PA745 Rack Mount Kit?
Id: 2817
Legacy Id: 2927

Question:
What is supplied in the PA745 Rack Mount Kit?

Answer:

	As of April 2003, the PA745 contains the following:
	
	1 - Link Bar
	1 - Long Mounting Bracket
	1 - Short Mounting Bracket
	1 - BNC to BNC cable (22 inches)
	1 - BNC to BNC bulkhead adapter
	1 - Package of mounting screws, plastic washers, and a plastic plug for the hole in the mounting bracket.
