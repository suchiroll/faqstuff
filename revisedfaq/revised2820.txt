Title: UHF Wireless Frequency Bands - United States
Id: 2820
Legacy Id: 2930

Question:
Would you tell me the RF Carrier Frequency ranges in the US for the following

receivers?


UHF Series

ULX

UC


Answer:

	In April 2003:
	
	UHF Series: UA Band (782 - 806 MHz); UB Band (692-716); M4 Band (662 - 692); J4 Band (554-584)
	
	ULX: M1 Band (662-698); J1 Band (554-590)
	
	UC: UA Band and UB Band (see above)
