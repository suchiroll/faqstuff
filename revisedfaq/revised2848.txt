Title: Theater Audio - Sewn-in holder for WL93
Id: 2848
Legacy Id: 2960

Question:
I'm interested in the WL93 for inconspicuous use on women's clothing.


Answer:

	We suggest that the loop portion of Velcro be sewn in the clothing. The hook portion is mated to the loop portion with the WL93 cable trapped between the two pieces of Velcro.
