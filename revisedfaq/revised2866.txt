Title: Global Scene Change for DFR11EQ
Id: 2866
Legacy Id: 2978

Question:
How can I make one DRS-10 switch control a global scene change on multiple DFR11EQ units?


Answer:

	1) In the DFR11EQ software, choose the Options menu and select Global Scene Mode.
	
	2) Interconnect all DFR11EQ units using the Shure Link ports on the rear panel.
	
	3) Connect the DRS-10 switch to any one of the linked DFR11EQ units.
