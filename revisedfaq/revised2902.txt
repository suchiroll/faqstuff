Title: Earphone Maximum Sound Pressure Level Output
Id: 2902
Legacy Id: 3026

Question:
What is the maximum sound pressure output of the E series earphones?

Answer:

	E1 and E1C - Max output = 117 dB SPL at 3% Total Harmonic Distortion
	
	E2 and E2C - Max output = 121 dB SPL at 3% Total Harmonic Distortion
	
	E3 and E3C - Max output = 117 dB SPL at 3% Total Harmonic Distortion
	
	E5 and E5C - Max output = 123 dB SPL at 3% Total Harmonic Distortion
