Title: Microphone M188H
Id: 2908
Legacy Id: 3032

Question:
I just picked up a Midco M188H Microphone manufactured by Shure around the late 1970's-early 1980's. Can you give me any info about it?


Answer:

	The M188H is the equivalent of the Shure model: 588-SA (no longer made). The closest current model is the Shure 588SDX.
