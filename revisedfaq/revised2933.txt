Title: Wiring Shure WL185 to AKG PT4000  Transmitter
Id: 2933
Legacy Id: 3059

Question:
How do I connect a Shure WL185 lavalier microphone to an AKG PT4000 transmitter?


Answer:

	Wire as follows:
	 
	
		
			
				Shure WL185
			
				AKG PT4000
		
		
			
				Red Wire
			
				Pin 3
		
		
			
				Black Wire
			
				Pin 2
		
		
			
				Shield
			
				Pin 1
		
		
			
				Connect a 10 kohm resistor between pins 1 and 2
