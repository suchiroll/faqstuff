Title: Internal wiring of the Beta 58 XLR connector
Id: 3
Legacy Id: 3

Question:
What is the correct internal wiring of the Beta 58 XLR connector?


Answer:

	The Beta 58 XLR connector should be wired as follows:
	
	Pin 1 -- Shield/Chassis
	Pin 2 -- Green
	Pin 3 -- Yellow
	
	The pin numbers are on the XLR connector.
