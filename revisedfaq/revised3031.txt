Title: UT Wireless Series - Discontinuation
Id: 3031
Legacy Id: 3161

Question:
When was the the UT Series discontinued? And what replaced it?


Answer:

	The UT wireless series was discontinued in January 2005. It was replaced by the PGX wireless series. Note that a PGX transmitter cannot be used with a UT receiver, nor can a UT transmitter be used with a PGX receiver.
	
	The PGX Series was discontinued in May 2013. The BLX Series replaced the PGX Series.
