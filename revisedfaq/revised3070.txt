Title: Differences between the UT and PGX lavalier systems
Id: 3070
Legacy Id: 3201

Question:
What are the specific differences between the UT presenter system and the PGX system? Which is better?


Answer:

	PGX is superior.
	
	1) PGX has multiple built-in frequencies.
	
	2) PGX receiver scans to find optimal frequency.
	
	3) PGX receiver sets PGX transmitter via infra-red signal.
	
	4) PGX has longer battery life.
	
	5) PGX has better audio quality.
