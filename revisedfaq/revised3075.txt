Title: FP22 Headphone Amplifier Discontinuation
Id: 3075
Legacy Id: 3206

Question:
What is the status of the Shure FP22 headphone amplifier?


Answer:

	As of March 2005, the FP22 headphone amplifier is discontinued and inventory is depleted.
	
	Battery powered headphone amplifiers are manufactured by:
	
	Sound Devices
	www.sounddevices.com
	
	Whirlwind Audio
	www.whirlwindusa.com
	
	Rolls
	www.rolls.com
