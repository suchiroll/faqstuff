Title: Alternate Mounting Clips for the WL93 and SM93
Id: 3080
Legacy Id: 3211

Question:
Are there other mounting clips available that will work with the WL93?


Answer:

	The mounting clips available for the WL50, WL51, MC50, and MC51 will work with the WL93 and SM93. There are four clips available: a pin mount, a magnet mount, a tie bar mount for one mic, and a tie bar mount for two mics.
