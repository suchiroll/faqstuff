Title: Length of a record groove - 33 1/3 rpm long-playing record
Id: 3125
Legacy Id: 3259

Question:
Could you tell me what the approximate length of the groove on one side of a 12" Long play (usually black) vinyl 33 1/3 rpm record of the 1960's - 1970's era ?


Answer:

	An average length is 1,500 feet or about 500 meters.
