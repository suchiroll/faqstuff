Title: External MS matrix for VP88
Id: 3136
Legacy Id: 3270

Question:
The VP88 User Guide mentions the use of an "external matrix" to decode an MS recording into Left-Right stereo. Does Shure sell an external matrix?


Answer:

	Shure does not sell an external matrix.

	Here is one company that does: http://www.peterengh.com
This matrix is called the Sonic Orbit.
