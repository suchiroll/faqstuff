Title: Jumper X203 for monaural output of SCM262
Id: 3248
Legacy Id: 3401

Question:
Where is jumper point X203 in the SCM262?


Answer:

	The jumper X203 is located on the upper side of the printed circuit broad, an inch in front of the #3 channel RCA jack inputs. Place a solder blob across the two solder points within the square marked as X203.
