Title: Specifications for a Shure R47XT
Id: 33
Legacy Id: 33

Question:
Could you tell me the specs for a Shure R47XT?


Answer:

	Output (1kHz at 5 cm/sec peak recorded velocity): 5.0 mV
	Frequency Response: 20-20kHz
	Channel Balance: within 2 dB
	Channel Separation (minimum): 25 dB at 1,000 Hz
	Tracking Force: 3/4 to 1 1/2 grams
	Trackability @ 1 gram (cm/sec peak recorded velocity):
	400 Hz - 22 cm/sec
	1,000 Hz - 33 cm/sec
	10,000 Hz - 19 cm/sec
