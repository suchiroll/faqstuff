Title: V15RS replacement stylus
Id: 3321
Legacy Id: 3493

Question:
The stylus on my V15 RS cartridge broke, finally. Can I obtain a replacement?

Answer:

	Styli that will fit the V15RS: VNSE3HE; or N111E; or VN5MR
	
	Shure no longer has stock of these models. Many sources where you may purchase discontinued Shure styli.
