Title: What does "NEF" stand for in E3CNEFS?
Id: 3355
Legacy Id: 3539

Question:
What is the NEF designation ?



Answer:

	E3 = model of the transducer
	C = sold via Consumer outlets
	N = black plastic (to match the Nano iPod)
	EFS = English/French/Spanish on the package
	
	Basically, E3 earbuds in black.
