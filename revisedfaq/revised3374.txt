Title: Repair of I2c-M
Id: 3374
Legacy Id: 3561

Question:
I have an I2c-M that I need repaired. Where do I send it?


Answer:

	Take your broken I2c-M to the AT store and they will be able to replace it for you.
	
	Do not return the I2c-M to Shure, as we do not have the capability to replace it. The I2c-M needs to be replaced through AT
