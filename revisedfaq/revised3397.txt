Title: Cable for SM98 and WM98
Id: 3397
Legacy Id: 3587

Question:
What is the part number for the 3' 8" (1.1 meter) audio cable that connects the SM98 or WM98 to a Shure wireless transmitter? This cable has a 3 pin TA4F connector on the mic end and a TA4F connector on the transmitter end.


Answer:

	The Shure part number for this cable is 90A4039.
