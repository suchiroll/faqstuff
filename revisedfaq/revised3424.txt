Title: MX1BP Discontinuation
Id: 3424
Legacy Id: 3615

Question:
When was the MX1BP discontinued?


Answer:

	Discontinuation date: May 31, 2008
	Introduced: 1999
	Final U.S. retail price: $184.54
	Description: Preamplifier for the MX series of lavalier microphones; operates from phantom power or a 9Vdc battery; 4 pin TA4M input connector; power-on LED; audio mute switch; 12 foot (4 meter) attached cable with male XLR at equipment end
