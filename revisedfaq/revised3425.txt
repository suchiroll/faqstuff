Title: P4800 Discontinuation and Suggested Replacement
Id: 3425
Legacy Id: 3616

Question:
When was the P4800 discontinued?


Answer:

	Discontinuation date: April 30, 2008
	
	Introduced: 2001
	
	Final U.S. retail price: $2,665
	
	Description: 4 in / 8 out digital audio system processor; one rack space; Windows-based computer required to program the audio routing and to set the internal processors, such as equalizers, crossovers, limiters, feedback reducers, et al.
	
	Companies that make similar devices:
	Biamp
	Rane
	Symetrix
	Yamaha
	Ashly
	dbx
	BSS Audio
