Title: WCM16 wiring to TA4F
Id: 3430
Legacy Id: 3621

Question:
What is the wiring scheme for the Shure WCM16 headworn mic?


Answer:

	Cable shield to TA4F connector pin 1 [return for audio and bias]

	Red wireto TA4F connector pin 2 [+5 Vdc bias]

	Blackwireto TA4F connector pin3 [audio]

	Jumper between TA4F connector pin 3 and pin 4
