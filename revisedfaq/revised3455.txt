Title: P6R Antenna Connector
Id: 3455

Question:
What is the part number for the antenna connector that fastens to the P6R receiver?

Answer:

	The Shure part number for the entire P6R antenna was PA710.  The connector is made by Lemo and the Lemo part number is:

	FVG.00.250.NTCY

	http://www.lemo.com/partnum_search.do
