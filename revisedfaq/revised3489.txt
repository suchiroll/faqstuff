Title: MX392, MX412D DIP switch detailed drawing
Id: 3489
Legacy Id: 3688

Question:
Is a more detailed diagram available that shows the settings for the Option DIP Switches in the MX392 and MX412D microphones?



Answer:

	Factory Default Settings

	
		All DIP switches off
	
		Push-to-mute, momentary
	
		Muting performed in microphone


	

	

	The dip switches may have a transluscent orange plastic film covering them. Peel off the orange film to flip the dip switches.

	

	** To check the microphone operation after changing the DIP switch setting, make certain to re-fasten the microphone bottom platein order tore-establish the mic circuit ground connection.
