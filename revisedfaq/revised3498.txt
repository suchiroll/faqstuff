Title: WCE6 headband / earclip
Id: 3498

Question:
Is there an accessory that will help hold my WCE6 headworn mic more securely?


Answer:

	Yes, the Countryman "E6-Ear Clip" accessory.  The "E6-Ear Clip" will securely hold the WCE6 to a person's head and prevent the microphone from falling off in most circumstances.www.countryman.com/
