Title: MX202 Rubber Grommet
Id: 3527
Legacy Id: 3731

Question:
What is the part number for the rubber grommet supplied with the MX202? The grommet fits through a hole in a ceiling tile, and secures the MX202 cable.


Answer:

	As of January 2009, the part number is 66A409. This part is defined as a slitted rubber grommet. Note that such part numbers are subject to frequent change.
