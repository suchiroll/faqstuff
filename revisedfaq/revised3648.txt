Title: Motorola Droid with the Shure SE115M
Id: 3648
Legacy Id: 3856

Question:
Will the Motorola Droid work with the Shure SE115M? Can I listen to music and talk on the phone?


Answer:

	Yes, the SE115M will work with the Motorola Droid for music and for phone calls. 
	In addition, the Droid will also work using standard Shure earphones and the discontinued Shure MPA-3C; (MPA =Music Phone Adapter). 
	Also, standard Shure earphones will work with the Droid if listening to music is the only function required.
