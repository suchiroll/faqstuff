Title: PSM700 Series Discontinuation
Id: 3673
Legacy Id: 3882

Question:
When was the PSM700 series discontinued?


Answer:

	The P7T transmitter and and P7R receiver were discontinued in May 2010.  The PSM700 was replaced by the Shure PSM900 series.The final U.S. retail prices were:
	P7T - $1,326
	P7R - $1,218
