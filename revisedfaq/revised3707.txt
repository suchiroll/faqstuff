Title: SCL2 Discontinuation
Id: 3707
Legacy Id: 3935

Question:
When were the SCL2 earphones discontinued?


Answer:

	The SCL2-K (black) earphones and SCL2-CL (clear) earphones were discontinued in July 2010; replaced by SE115 earphones.
	The final U.S. retail price for the SCL2-K or SCL2-CL was $124.00.
