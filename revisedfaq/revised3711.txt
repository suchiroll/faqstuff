Title: Cable Wiring for Beta 91
Id: 3711
Legacy Id: 3944

Question:
Do have a wire diagram for a Beta 91 Cable? My cable is defective on the 4 pin end. I have a new 4 pin connector, but don't know how it should be wired.


Answer:

	Pin 1 onboth connectors:cable shield (ground for bias and audio)
	
	Pin 2 onboth connectors:red wire(bias)
	
	Pin 3 onboth connectors:black wire (audio)
	
	Pin 4 - not used
