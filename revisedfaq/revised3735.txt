Title: Discontinuation - models RS25, RS35, RS45
Id: 3735
Legacy Id: 3969

Question:
When were these microphones discontinued? What replaced these models?


Answer:

	The RS25, RS35, and RS45 microphones were discontinued in August 2010. The RS25 was replaced by the model SV100.
	The RS45 was replaced by the model SV200.
	The RS35 was not replaced by a new model.
