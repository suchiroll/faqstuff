Title: DFR22 Hard Reset
Id: 3758
Legacy Id: 3994

Question:
How to I reset the DFR22 back to the factory supplied settings?


Answer:

	NOTE: This procedure will clear all the presets programmed by the user. It is recommended to save the presets onto a computer before initiating the hard reset.
	
	To perform a hard reset:

	Power off the DFR22.
	Hold down the following front panel buttons in "Row A": [Press to Lock/Hold to Clear] and [Bypass DFR Filters] - then power up the DFR22.
	The preset indicator will count down as the reset takes place.
	Release the buttons once countdown begins.
