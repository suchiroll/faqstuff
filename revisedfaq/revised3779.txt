Title: A13HD Mounting Flange
Id: 3779
Legacy Id: 4015

Question:
Please provide basic dimensions for the A13HD mounting flange.


Answer:

	The A13HD is metal and has these characteristics:
	Diameter: 3.0 inches
	Total height: 1.312 inch
	Base thickness: 0.52 inch
	Length of 5/8-27 external thread: 0.792 inch
	Three holes for mounting screws are located every 120 degrees and 1.0 inch out from center.
