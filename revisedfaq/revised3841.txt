Title: AKG Mic to Shure Bodypack Transmitter
Id: 3841
Legacy Id: 4081

Question:
What is the method recommended by AKG to connect one of their condenser lapel mics into a Shure bodypack transmitter using a TA4F connector?



Answer:

	According to a technical bulletin from AKG, dated January 2007:
	AKG red wire to Shure TA4F pin 3 and pin 4
	AKG shield wire to Shure TA4F pin 1
	8,200 ohm resistor between Shure TA4F pin 3 and pin 2
	
	Note: Shure has not tested the method above.
