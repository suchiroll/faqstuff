Title: Shure Lapel Mic into an AKG Pocket Transmitter
Id: 3842
Legacy Id: 4082

Question:
What is the method recommended by AKG to connect a Shure condenser lapel mic into one of their pocket transmitters?



Answer:

	According to a technical bulletin from AKG, dated January 2007:
	Shurered wire to AKG pin 3
	Shure black wire to AKG pin 2
	Shure shield wire to AKG pin 1
	
	Note: Shure has not tested the method above.
