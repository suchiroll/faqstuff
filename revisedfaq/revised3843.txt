Title: PGX4 mounted to URT
Id: 3843
Legacy Id: 4083

Question:
I have a PGX4 receiver and a Shure URT (universal rack tray), but I have misplaced the four screws that fasten the receiver to the rack tray. Please provide the screw specifications.


Answer:

	Machine screw, hex head, plated steel.  
	Size: M4 x .7   (metric with a thread length of 0.7 cm or 7 mm)
	Shure part number 30B8190B - as of January 2011
