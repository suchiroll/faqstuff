Title: Earpad  material for SRH440
Id: 3882
Legacy Id: 4128

Question:
I would like to know what material the ear cushions of the SRH440 are made of. Leather or leather-like?


Answer:

	The SRH440 ear pads are made of polyurethane foam; the outer skin is made of “leatherette” which is a synthetic fiber covered with a soft PVC layer.
