Title: MX405 / 410 / 415 LED Options
Id: 3892
Legacy Id: 4138

Question:
What LED options are available on these models?


Answer:

	MX405 or MX410 or MX415
	Bi-color LED ring located at the base of the gooseneck, where it connects to the preamp.
	ON indication is Green. OFF indication is Red.
	
	MX405R or MX410R or MX415R
	Red LED ring located near the mic element.
	ON indication is Red. OFF indication is Flashing Red or Dark (user selectable).
