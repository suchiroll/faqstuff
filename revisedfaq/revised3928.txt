Title: Rubber plug for MX400SMP
Id: 3928
Legacy Id: 4177

Question:
What are the dimensions of the rubber cap/plug supplied with the MX400SMP? It is used to protect the connector opening when a mic is not attached to the MX400SMP.


Answer:

	The part number for this plug is 66A11907. This circular plug/cap is black rubber.It protects the MX400SMP connector opening when a mic is not connected.

	

	The diameter of the plug top is0.797 inch.The plug top has a gentle radius, i.e., it is rounded.

	

	The diameter of the lower part that fits into the MX400SMP connector opening is 0.627 inch.

	

	The total length is 0.349 inch.
