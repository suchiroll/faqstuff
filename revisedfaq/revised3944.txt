Title: AXT620 number of ports
Id: 3944
Legacy Id: 4193

Question:
How many networked devices can be directly connected to the AXT620 ethernet switch?


Answer:

	The AXT630 has 8 ports on the rear and one port on the front, for a total of nine devices.
	
	For more than nine devices, multiple AXT630 ethernet switches can be connected together.
