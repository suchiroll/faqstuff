Title: UR transmitter - " BL " on LCD display?
Id: 3983
Legacy Id: 4280

Question:
" BL " shows on the LCD display of my UR2 transmitter. It isnext to the battery icon. What does it mean?


Answer:

	BL means that the transmitter has been Band Limited. The UR transmitter has been set to operate in a smaller frequency range than its full capability. This may be done to meet governmental regulations or for local operational reasons.
