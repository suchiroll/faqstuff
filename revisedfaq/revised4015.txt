Title: Error installing Wireless Workbench 5.0.5 on Windows 7
Id: 4015
Legacy Id: 4422

Question:
I am trying to install Wireless Workbench version 5.0.5 on a computer with Windows 7 and I get an error message.



Answer:

	Try running the installer in compatiblity mode. Right click on the installer and click Check Compatiblity.
