Title: V15LT with N97LT stylus?
Id: 4029
Legacy Id: 4439

Question:
Would it be possible for you to tell me if the N97LT stylus will fit your V15LT cartridge? Both appear the same except for their color (and tip shape).


Answer:

	Sorry, it does not fit.The N97LT stylus shank housing is too large to fit the V15LT stylus opening.
