Title: Self-muting 1/4" phone plug
Id: 4038
Legacy Id: 4470

Question:
I want to add a self-muting 1/4" phone plug to the cable that connects my guitar to the Shure wireless bodypack. I want to unplug my transmitter from one guitar and move it to another guitar, but not create buzz/hum/noise. Do you have a recommendation?


Answer:

	Neutrik manufactures an superb self-muting 1/4 phone plug: model NP2X-AU-SILENT
	
	To create your own cable:
	Phone plug sleeve  cable shield  TA4F pin 1
	Phone plug tip  cable inner conductor  TA4F pin 3
