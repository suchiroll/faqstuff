Title: Axient AMX/Crestron Control Strings
Id: 4145
Legacy Id: 5047

Question:
What are the control strings for the Axient system to control it via AMX or Crestron?


Answer:

	On the Axient system, the AXT400 receiver and AXT900 battery charger can be monitored and controlled via a control system, such as AMX, Crestron, or Extron.
	
	Axient Control Sting Document(pdf)
	
	The AMX or Crestron can control and/or monitor the following items:

	
		Mute
	
		Volume
	
		Names
	
		Frequency
	
		Battery Usage Information
	
		Interference Detection
	
		Audio  RF Meters
	
		Battery Charge Information
