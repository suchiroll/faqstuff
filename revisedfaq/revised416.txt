Title: ST6000 Information
Id: 416
Legacy Id: 423

Question:
Please provide information about the ST6000.



Answer:

	In brief, the ST6000 (discontinued in the early 1990's) is a teleconferencing mixer that requires for proper operation: an analog phone line connection and special two-element microphones. The ST6000 is only useful for teleconferencing, and not any other type of audio mixing.
