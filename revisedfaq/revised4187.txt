Title: SM93 into an iPhone
Id: 4187
Legacy Id: 5140

Question:
Willthe SM93 lav mic interface to an iPhone 5 with a Tascam iXZ via XLR?


Answer:

	Yes, it should work.The SM93 must connect into the female XLR connector on the Tascam iXZ, and phantom power must be activated on the Tascam device.
