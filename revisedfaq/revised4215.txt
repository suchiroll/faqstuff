Title: MX153 with Axient bodypack transmitter
Id: 4215
Legacy Id: 5185

Question:
Will the Shure MX153 work with the Axient AXT100 bodypack transmitter?


Answer:

	Sorry, it will not work. The Axient transmitter provides 7 to 9 volts of bias. The MX153 will not operate properly with bias above 5 volts.
