Title: A2WS set screw
Id: 4223
Legacy Id: 5205

Question:
I lost the set screw for the A2WS windscreen. What is the Shure part number? What is the sizing of the screw?


Answer:

	Shure part: 30A868 (December 2012)
	
	Sizing: #8-32 UNC-2B; length 0.187(4.75 mm); oval point; steel; black oxide finish
