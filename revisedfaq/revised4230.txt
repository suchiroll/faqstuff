Title: Discontinuation of the Shure M44-7-H
Id: 4230
Legacy Id: 5214

Question:
When was the M44-7-H discontinuned?


Answer:

	January 2013. The final U.S. retail price was $125. The M44-7-H is a Shure M44-7 cartridge mounted into a headshell for a Technics turntable. There is no recommended direct replacement in the Shure phono line.
	
	Note: The Shure M44-7 cartridge was not discontinued.
