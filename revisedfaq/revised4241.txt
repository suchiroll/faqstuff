Title: Rechargeable Battery Pack for DIS Infrared Receivers
Id: 4241
Legacy Id: 5274

Question:
What is the model number for the rechargeable battery pack used in the DIS Infrared receiver?


Answer:

	There are different IR receivers, depending on how many channels of reception are needed - DR6004, DR6008, or DR6032. The model number is printed on the battery door on the back of the receiver.
	
	The NiMH rechargeable battery pack used for the infrared receivers is BP6001.
