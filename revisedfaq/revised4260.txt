Title: Engrave a KSM9 microphone
Id: 4260
Legacy Id: 5317

Question:
My boss wants me to engrave our new KSM9's with a company ID. Will this damage the mic?


Answer:

	Make certain that the metal shavings do not get into the grill or mic element.Recommend that the mic element and grill be covered in plastic during the engraving.
	
	The vibration from the engraving will not damage the mic.
